    Mark your calendars! The MIDFLORIDA Event Center will partner with Budweiser and Southern Eagle Distributing to present the Port St. Lucie Country Music Tribute Bacon and BBQ Festival October 8-9. Boasting a lineup of nine national country music tribute bands, bacon and barbecue, country and Western vendors, a kids zone, and more, the weekend-long event promises fun for the whole family.

    The outdoor festivities kick off at 11 a.m. on October 8 featuring the following bands: Wide Open: The Jason Aldean Experience; Beer for My Horses: The Toby Keith Tribute Show; The Ultimate Tim McGraw Tribute Show; and Pirate Flag: Kenny Chesney Tribute Band.

    Beginning at noon on October 9, catch concerts by the following bands: Country Stars Tribute to Shania Twain; Walk the Line: Tribute to Johnny Cash; Country Stars Tribute to Garth Brooks; and “All My Rowdy Friends” The Ultimate Hank Williams Jr. Tribute.

    Throughout the weekend, Them Sweeney Boys, a country comedy revue, will entertain the crowd with their family-friendly hilarity. To fuel the fun, a lineup of food trucks and vendors will be on site with bacon, barbecue, and Southern-style cuisine. For a little retail therapy, vendors from across Florida will showcase their wares, including fine art, jewelry, clothing, unique gifts, and handcrafted items.

    The festival will also feature a dedicated kids zone, complete with a giant Jenga game, corn hole, inflatable baseball pitch, an obstacle course, rock climbing slide, a petting zoo, and more.

    Tickets are $12 per day day. Guests can also purchase a two-day ticket for $20. Children ages 12 and younger are free. Parking is free. Tickets are on sale now here or at the MIDFLORIDA Event Center Box Office, 9221 SE Event Center Place, Monday through Friday, from 9 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. Tickets may also be purchased by calling 800-514-3849.

    For more details, click here, call 772-807-4488, or email contact@midfloridaeventcenter.com.
