    Brock Purdy is an American football quarterback for the San Francisco 49ers of the National Football League (NFL). He was born in Queen Creek, Arizona, on December 27, 1999. Known as Mr. Irrelevant, Purdy played college football at Iowa State University.

    He rose to prominence after the 49ers selected him with the final pick in the 2022 NFL Draft, making him Mr. Irrelevant. Due to his fame, fans are curious about who Brock Purdy’s wife is.

    While Brock Purdy’s wife does not exist yet, his girlfriend, Jenna Brandt, has been the talk of the town, especially after the couple recently announced their engagement. The engagement news has left fans wondering: Is the famous NFL player married? Who is his wife?

    Here’s everything we know about Brock Purdy’s wife-to-be.

    Who Is Brock Purdy’s Girlfriend, Jenna Brandt?

    Like Purdy, Brandt is 23 years old. She was born on October 26, 1999. The famous NFL player’s wife-to-be, Jenna Brandt, grew up in Sumner, Iowa, with her parents, Kevin and Amy Brant, and three siblings, Kaylyn, Isaiah, and Morgan. She has a close-knit family and often features them on her Instagram account.

    Like her boyfriend-turned-fiance, Brandt is also an athlete. She is an avid volleyball player. Brock Purdy’s wife-to-be attended Sumner-Fredericksburg High School, where she played volleyball throughout her stay and was a two-time team captain. In addition to playing on a club volleyball team, Brandt also competed in track and field. 

    After graduating high school in 2018, Brandt enrolled at Iowa State in Ames, Iowa, where she majored in Kinesiology. She also joined Iowa State University’s volleyball team. Although there are no details about where the lovebirds met, Purdy joined Iowa State that same year, simultaneously making him and Brandt freshmen. 

    Hence, fans believe that is likely where they first met. Throughout college, Brandt continued her volleyball career as a setter for the Iowa State Cyclones. Also, she tried out for the U.S. Collegiate National Team and the USA Women’s Volleyball Team during her first year. 

    In late 2020, Brandt transferred to the University of Northern Iowa (UNI), where she wrapped up her collegiate volleyball career in 2022. Brandt thanked UNI for the “life-changing experience” and expressed her gratitude for the “opportunities, experiences, and friendships” she made at Iowa State.

    Brock Purdy’s Wife: How Did He Meet Jenna Brandt?

    Although it’s unclear when the couple started dating, many believe Brandt and Purdy were college sweethearts, as they enrolled in Iowa State the same year. However, the couple has not disclosed how they met due to their cagey lifestyle. 

    They became Instagram official in November 2022. As a testament to their strong relationship, Brock Purdy’s wife-to-be shared photos featuring Purdy and her family spending time together on social media.

    Is Brock Purdy Single?

    No, he is not single. The famous NFL player has been in a relationship with fellow athlete Jenna Brandt for at least a year. Although she has not earned the title of Brock Purdy’s wife, the lovebirds took their relationship to the next level when the NFL star got on one knee and popped the question. 

    Purdy proposed to Brandt on a dock in Saint Cloud, Florida, on July 2, 2023. Sharing photos of their engagement on Instagram, he wrote, “My Jenna girl forever. I can’t wait to be your husband and grow together in Christ. Here’s to forever, babe. I love you, J.B.!”

    In a separate Instagram post to announce the engagement, Brock Purdy’s wife-to-be wrote, “WE GET TO DO LIFE TOGETHER…FOREVER! Brock Purdy – I love everything about you & your heart for Jesus more than any words can say.”
