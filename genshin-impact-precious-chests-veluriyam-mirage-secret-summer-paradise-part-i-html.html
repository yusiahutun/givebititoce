    [V3.8 Event Guide] Precious Chests – Veluriyam Mirage (Secret Summer Paradise: Part I)

    Overview

    • Total (Part I): 12
    • Voyeux Vouchers: 12

    Note: All Chests give 1 Voyeux Voucher.

    Silver Bottle Courtyard

    #1 – Trapped Chest

    Challenge – Find the 3 hidden Hydro Eidolons

    Location: In front of the tents

    Press ‘Read’ on the Bulletin Board to start the Eidolon Hunter Challenge. Press ‘Found You!’ on the Slime Crate on top of the tent to discover Hydro Eidolon #1.

    Hydro Eidolon #1 – Slime Crate on Tent

    Press ‘Found You!’ on the small crate on top of the Slime Crate to discover Hydro Eidolon #2.

    Hydro Eidolon #2 – Small Crate on Slime Crate

    Press ‘Found You!’ on the broken vase on the fence discover Hydro Eidolon #3, completing the Challenge and freeing the chest.

    Hydro Eidolon #3 – Broken Vase on Fence

    #2 – Trapped Chest

    Challenge – Find the 3 hidden Hydro Eidolons

    Location: In front of the tents

    Press ‘Read’ on the Bulletin Board to start the Eidolon Hunter Challenge. Press ‘Found You!’ on the barrel to the right to discover Hydro Eidolon #1.

    Hydro Eidolon #1 – Right Barrel

    Press ‘Found You!’ on the Cryo Exploding Mine on the door to discover Hydro Eidolon #2.

    Hydro Eidolon #2 – Cryo Exploding Mine on Door

    Press ‘Found You!’ on the Magical Crystal Ore on top of the roof to discover Hydro Eidolon #3, completing the Challenge and freeing the chest.

    Hydro Eidolon #3 – Magical Crystal Ore on Roof

    #3 – Spawned Chest

    Time Trial Challenge – Collect 20 Dendro Particles within 180s

    Location (Challenge Marker): On top of the hill

    After collecting 7 Dendro Particles, defeat 3 Floating Dendro Fungi to destroy the bubble. Press ‘Summon Dendrogranum’ and hit the Clusterleaf of Cultivation to spawn Four-Leaf Sigils. Teleport to those to continue.

    Note: You cannot clear out the enemies in advance before the Time Trial Challenge as they and the bubble will respawn.

    At 19 Dendro Particles, defeat the 3 Hilichurls to destroy the bubble. Collect the 20th Dendro Particle to complete the Time Trial Challenge and spawn the chest.

    Overgrown Valley

    #4 – Trapped Chest

    Challenge – Find the 3 hidden Hydro Eidolons

    Location: Ground level, by the water

    Press ‘Read’ on the Bulletin Board to start the Eidolon Hunter Challenge. Press ‘Found You!’ on the Dusty Balloon to discover Hydro Eidolon #1.

    Hydro Eidolon #1 – Dusty Balloon

    Press ‘Found You!’ on the Slime Crate to discover Hydro Eidolon #2.

    Hydro Eidolon #2 – Slime Crate

    Press ‘Found You!’ on the small crate on top of the leaf to discover Hydro Eidolon #3, completing the Challenge and freeing the chest.

    Hydro Eidolon #3 – Small Crate on Leaf

    #5 – Spawned Chest

    Challenge – Destroy 65 Bloatty Floatties

    Location (Challenge Marker): On the Choo-Choo tracks

    Ride the Choo-Choo Cart to start the Challenge and complete it to spawn the chest.

    #6 – Spawned Chest

    Time Trial Challenge – Collect 30 Dendro Particles within 180s

    Location (Challenge Marker): On the ledge

    Complete the Time Trial Challenge to spawn the chest.

    #7 – Spawned Chest

    Challenge – Destroy 100 Bloatty Floatties

    Location (Challenge Marker): On the Choo-Choo tracks

    Ride the Choo-Choo Cart to start the Challenge and complete it to spawn the chest.

    #8 – Spawned Chest

    Hydro Monument Puzzle

    Location (Chest): Ground level

    Press ‘Please help!’ on the Hydro Eidolon to show the correct order, and then activate the 5 Hydro Monuments with Hydro to spawn the chest.

    #9-10 Requirement*

    * The Hydro Eidolon to fill the Flower with water, spawning a Tracklayer Ring. Ride the Choo-Choo Cart and collect the 3 Tracklayer Fruits using Jubilant Waves to spawn new tracks. Switch over to the new tracks to reach the location.

    #9 – Spawned Chest

    Challenge – Destroy 65 Bloatty Floatties

    Location (Challenge Marker): In the cave, on the Choo-Choo tracks

    Ride the Choo-Choo Cart to start the Challenge and complete it to spawn the chest.

    #10 – Spawned Chest

    Challenge – Destroy 40 Bloatty Floatties and 1 Fungus Balloon

    Location (Challenge Marker): In the cave, on the Choo-Choo tracks

    Ride the Choo-Choo Cart to start the Challenge and complete it to spawn the chest.

    #11 Requirements

    Set the track to the the Purple Flower (desert). Ride the Choo-Choo Cart and collect the 3 Tracklayer Fruits using Jubilant Waves to spawn new tracks. Switch over to the new tracks to reach the location.

    #11 – Spawned Chest

    Challenge – Destroy 90 Bloatty Floatties and 3 Fungus Balloons

    Location (Challenge Marker): On the Choo-Choo tracks

    Ride the Choo-Choo Cart to start the Challenge and complete it to spawn the chest.

    #12 Requirements

    Complete: World Quest – A Starry Night, as Remembered

    Location (World Quest):

    #12 – Spawned Chest

    World Quest Chest

    Location (Chest):

    Complete the World Quest to spawn the chest.
