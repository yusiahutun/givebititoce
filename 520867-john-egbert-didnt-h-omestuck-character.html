    Quick facts about John Egbert from Homestuck

    The character is often remembered as the Heir of Breath or the boy with the windy powers. He has more to his story than meets the eye. John Egbert's personality is depicted with loyalty and genuine care for his friends. Here are some facts about him:

    1. John's arrival on earth led to his mother's death

    John arrived on earth as an infant travelling by meteor, which resulted in the death of his ecto-biological mother. The child was adopted and raised by his genetic half-brother as his dad.

    His father generally supported John's interests and began showing an interest in the harlequins that John was drawing. He attempted to bond with John but failed to realize John's distaste for his drawings.

    2. The character used to draw harlequins as a child

    During John's childhood, Gamzee Makara used his chuckle voodoo on him. This caused a doll that resembled Jack Noir to appear in John's dream room on Prospit. It further made him write self-deprecating remarks, drawing harlequin pictures on his room's walls while still asleep.

    The teenager has dark brown hair that he maintains an upward style. He also wears a yellow suit with a moon figure as part of his Prospit outfit.

    John's grandma, Nanna Sprite, helps John by fighting the demons and goblins. She showed that she loved John by baking him cookies. Whenever John looks weak, she treats him to perfect health.
