    Golf player Michelle Wie won the US Women's Open on Sunday - and celebrated by drinking with her friends and twerking.

    Photos and videos of Wie, 24, partying after her victory at North Carolina club Pinehurst No.2 were shared on social media.

    Wie uploaded a photo of herself that was taken on Sunday night which shows her drinking out of the US Women's Open trophy.

    'So.....this happened last night.... #tabletopping #stillcantbelieveit' she captioned the photograph.

    An edited video has also emerged online, set to the DJ Snake & Lil Jon song 'Turn Down for What,' which shows Wie carrying her trophy through the room as onlookers take photographs -  before she is seen drinking out of the cup and twerking against a wall.
