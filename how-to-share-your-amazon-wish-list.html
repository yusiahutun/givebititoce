    How to Share Your Amazon Wish List

    Your Amazon wish lists are shareable, which means you can let people know what items you are looking to buy. You can even let people edit your lists if you want. Here's how to share your Amazon wish lists on desktop and mobile.

    Your Amazon wish lists are shareable, which means you can let people know what items you are looking to buy. You can even let people edit your lists if you want. Here's how to share your Amazon wish lists on desktop and mobile.

    Similar to sharing Google Docs, when you share a wish list, the list turns from "Private" to "Shared." You then get a link that you can share with people so they can view your wish list items.

    Related: How to Create and Better Manage Amazon Wish Lists

    Share Your Amazon Wish List on Desktop

    To share your wish lists from your Windows, Mac, Linux, or Chromebook computer, use the official Amazon website.

    Start by launching your web browser and opening the Amazon site. On the site, log in to your account.

    In the top-right corner of Amazon, hover your cursor over "Account & Lists."

    In the menu that opens, from the "Your Lists" section, select the wish list to share.

    On the wish list page, beneath the list name, click "Invite."

    You will see an "Invite Others to Your List" box. Here, if you want to invite people to see your list, click the "View Only" button. If you want people to see as well as edit your list, click the "View and Edit" option.

    We'll choose "View Only."

    In the same box, you will now see two more options.

    To copy the link to your list which you can then manually share with people, click the "Copy Link" option. To open your default email program and share the wish list link, click the "Invite by Email" option.

    If you have copied the link, you can now send it via email, text message, or however you want. The recipients can use this link to access your wish list on Amazon.

    Share Your Amazon Wish List on Mobile

    On your iPhone, iPad, or Android phone, use the Amazon app to share your wish lists.

    To do so, first, open the Amazon app on your phone. Make sure you are signed in to your account in the app.

    At the bottom of the app, tap the user icon.

    On the page that opens, at the top, tap "Your Lists."

    On the "List & Registry" page, in the "Your Lists" tab, tap the wish list to share.

    Tap "Invite."

    You will see an "Invite Someone To" menu.

    To let someone view your wishlist, tap "View Only" in the menu. To allow someone to view as well as edit the list, tap the "View and Edit" option.

    You now have various options to choose from to share your Amazon wish list. If you'd like to copy the link to your list so you can then manually share it, tap the "Copy Link" option.

    And that's it.

    We hope this helps you share your shortlisted items with people who can advise you on these products, and even help you buy them. Enjoy!
