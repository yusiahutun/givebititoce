25 Jul 2024, 00:00

For Dior spring/summer 2020, Maria Grazia Chiuri created an "inclusive garden" that was inspired by archival photographs of Catherine Dior, Christian Dior's sister, showing her surrounded by flowers in her garden, which was her passion. Catherine – whose nickname was Miss Dior and is who the house's first fragrance was named after – was an essential protagonist in the fashion house's history. The impact of this inspiration and of Catherine Dior's love of gardening specifically was felt throughout the collection (on floral-embroidered pieces, straw hats and textured raffia dresses) and through the impressive set, which included more than 100 trees.
