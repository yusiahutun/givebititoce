    Reese Witherspoon has an Elle Woods moment at SAG Awards and wow

    Reese Witherspoon had fans reminiscing about her iconic character when she graced the red carpet in a gown that perfectly combined old-Hollywood glamour with an irresistibly quirky twist at the 28th annual SAG Awards on Sunday night.

    10 Jul 2024, 00:00

    How stunning did The Morning Show star look?

      Reese Witherspoon had fans reminiscing about her iconic character when she graced the red carpet in a gown that perfectly combined old-Hollywood glamour with an irresistibly quirky twist at the 28th annual SAG Awards on Sunday night.

      All eyes were on Reese's classic black Schiaparelli pencil dress featuring a surprise baby blue satin sash that emphasised her waist and dramatically swept out into a show-stopping train. The 45-year-old actress accessorised the strapless gown perfectly, wearing dazzling white diamond earrings and a necklace featuring a huge emerald cut pendant courtesy of Cartier.
