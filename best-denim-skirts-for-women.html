    The fashion world’s obsession with denim is unwavering, but there’s been a swift return of long denim skirts, with midi and maxi styles seen all over the runways and our Instagram feeds.

    Altuzarra, Givenchy and Masha Popova had denim maxi skirts in their new season collections, and with the arrival of 2023, they’re now absolutely everywhere.

    First introduced in the 70s as an alternative to jeans, the denim skirt has been an on-off staple ever since. Most recently, more polished styles in longer lengths with front slit details are the look du jour.

    How to style a denim skirt

    Like your favourite pair of jeans, they’re so versatile. Go for true noughties nostalgia and wear a long denim skirt with ballet flats, a classic tee, and a leather jacket or for timeless styling add a white shirt and leather boots or slingback heels. If you really want to emulate the street style set, add a twist to the Canadian tux and wear your denim skirt with a matching denim jacket.

    Celebrities wearing denim skirts

    Spanish model Nuria Blanco styles her split-hem midi skirt with a cropped trench and knee-high leather boots. We've also spotted her wearing a denim mini with ankle socks and loafers.

    You may also like

    The eternally stylish Elsa Hosk shows us how it's done in a vintage denim maxi skirt by Saint Laurent. Featuring a panelled patchwork design, high waist and A-line shape, she went for a 70s style look with her oversized sunglasses and beige bomber jacket.  

    Oliver Palermo is the only proof we need that double denim can look chic. Her modern take on the Canadian Tux can be styled with a pair of heeled mules or knee-length boots as we move into the colder months. 

    How we chose the best denim skirts

    • Style: We've started our edit with the most popular cut right now - a fitted skirt falling to a midi length with a front split for movement. However, if you want something a little more unique, we've also included pieces with pleated designs and flared silhouettes. The denim mini never really goes out of style, so we've added a few of those too if you prefer something shorter.
    • Price: We've included mostly high street buys, which work out to be very affordable when you take cost per wear into consideration. We've also added a few more premium options around the £200 mark from the likes of Net-a-Porter and Reformation if you want to invest.
    • Inclusivity: We looked for denim skirts in a wide range of sizes, from 4-22 and XXS-4XL.

    The best denim skirts to shop now

    From high street favourites Mango and H&M to more premium labels Khaite and Reformation, we’ve found all of the best denim skirts available online. Scroll on to shop the edit.

      We love Mango’s denim midi skirt paired with sling-backs to look effortlessly put together this autumn. It has a straight mid-rise cut and also comes in black and off-white.

    • M&S Denim Midi Skirt

      Marks & Spencer's denim midi skirt is a high street bargain at less than £30. The bestselling piece has a flattering high-waist and stretch fabric, plus the coveted split hem.

    • Ro&Zo Denim Midi Skirt

      This denim midi skirt from Ro&Zo has a figure-hugging cut falling to a midi length with a sophisticated front split for easy movement. We love it styled with black leather ankle boots for autumn.

      Frankie Bridge has been spotted in this denim midi skirt from New Look. It comes in a high-waisted, A-line cut.

    • Khaite Levine Denim Midi Skirt

      This 90s-style midi skirt from Arket is made from stretchy recycled cotton and has a slim-fit silhouette. It features a slender denim waist belt with a D-ring buckle and a centre back slit.

    Denim maxi skirts

      Sustainably-made from 100% organic cotton, Aligne's denim maxi skirt has a contemporary A-line silhouette that's effortlessly cool for the office and beyond.

    Denim mini skirts

      & Other Stories' denim mini skirt has a high waist and slim fit with belt loops and five pockets - a classic wardrobe staple.

    • H&M Denim Mini Skirt

      With its button-down front and A-line silhouette, Boden’s preppy denim skirt is more unique than your standard mini.
