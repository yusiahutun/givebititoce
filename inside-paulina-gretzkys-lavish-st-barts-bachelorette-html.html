    Paulina Gretzky is one step closer to marrying her pro-golfer fiancé Dustin Johnson after enjoying a lavish bachelorette party in St Barts with her closest friends.  

    The 33-year-old, who is the daughter of retired ice hockey Hall of Famer Wayne Gretzky, has been engaged for nearly a decade and shares two children with her husband-to-be, but that didn't stop her from going out all out for her last hurrah. 

    Paulina was joined by eight of her closest pals — including her sister-in-law Sara Gretzky — when she flew to the French-speaking Caribbean island on a private jet filled with swag for her and her party guests.

    The bride-to-be donned a white strapless mini dress and sneakers while her friends wore shades of pink. They all topped off their looks with custom cowboy-style fedora hats by Caviilou that featured their initials in rhinestones.   

    The hats ordinarily cost $200 each, but they may have been a gift from her friend Kristina Melnichenko, who co-founded Caviilou with Bahar Boroujerdi. 

    Paulina documented her entire bachelorette weekend on her Instagram Stories last week, but she must have been feeling nostalgic on Tuesday when she shared a throwback photo of them boarding their private jet. 

    She posed on the stairs by herself while her friends huddled together on the tarmac together. She captioned the picture 'P's Last D,' which was also the getaway's official hashtag.
