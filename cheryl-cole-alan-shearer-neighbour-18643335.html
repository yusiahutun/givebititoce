    Cheryl Cole to be Alan Shearer's neighbour in 6.75million mansion

    The former X Factor judge is looking to move to an area dubbed Millionaires Row by locals. Exclusive Darras Hall village near Newcastle is already home to footballers Alan Shearer, 42, and Peter Beardsley, 52.

    26 May 2024, 00:00

    The former X Factor judge is looking to move to an area dubbed Millionaires’ Row by locals.

    Exclusive Darras Hall village near Newcastle is already home to footballers Alan Shearer, 42, and Peter Beardsley, 52.

    And now the price of Runnymede Mansion has dropped by £2m.

    Estate agent Ben Pridden said: “It is as impressive a house that exists in that location. It has fabulous entertaining space with all the requirements of a pop diva.

    “It has a swimming pool for parties and of course for keeping fit. It also has a private gym and cinema for watching back her latest videos.”

    The home is around a 20-minute drive from Byker where Cheryl, 30, grew up.
