The couple left the show as lovers but announced their break up months later via social media. When season 4 commenced, the pair were present and back together. However, after some time, they broke up again. So, what happened? And are Mitch and Tina still together?

    Tina Provis and Mitch Hibberd announced their second split in mid-December 2022. The breakup came months after reuniting on Love Island Australia season four. Their separation shocked viewers who thought that the couple would rekindle their love again. The revelation has led to inquiries from fans. So, what happened to the winning couple from Love Island season three?

    Who are Mitch and Tina from Love Island?

    Tina Provis is a former contestant in the Love Island series and appeared in seasons three and four. She hails from Sydney, Australia, where she works as a PR consultant. Tina is also a content creator and has a self-titled YouTube channel where she vlogs about her daily activities.

    Are Mitch and Tina still together?

    Unfortunatley no. The 2021 Love Island Australia winners called it quits for the second time on 15 December 2022. The pair were the hottest couple during the show's third season, bagging an overall price of $50,000. However, after the show concluded, Mitch and Tina returned to real life, and things weren't easy.

    Why did Mitch and Tina break up?

    According to the couple, the relationship failed because of various issues, including distance. Tina and Mitch live far apart, with one residing in Sydney and the other in Melbourne.

    Mitch's version

    Mitch explained via social media that he and Tina continued to see each other after coming from Spain, where Love Island season 4 was. He added that they dated for a few months, but the long-distance relationship became challenging.

    Tina's version
