    The TV presenter showed us how to do partywear!

      TV presenter Caroline Flack posted a smouldering shot on her Instagram account showcasing her incredibly lean, toned and tanned legs! The picture appeared to have had the special effect placed on it, known as Kira Kira, which a whole host of celebrities have been enjoying, including Holly Willoughby. The app made the monochrome mini dress totally dazzle in the light! The dress that the TV presenter was wearing was from high end designer Hasan Hejazi, and is priced at £275.

      Caroline enhanced the snap with popular sparkly app Kira Kira

      She teamed the stunning ensemble with a high-street gem; a pair of velvet block heels from none other than luxury shoe specialist Dune London, which retail at £80. Luckily for fans of the presenter, all items are in stock online, in all sizes.

      Caroline's platforms are by Dune London, and priced at £80

      The classic retro style mini dress boasts two textures; a fitted jersey bodice with long sleeves, and a statement striped sequined panel that is lined in luxury satin. The dress actually comes with a detachable stretch satin gold belt, which Caroline has chosen to incorporate into her sultry stance!

      Exclusive: Caroline Flack reveals the secrets to her healthy lifestyle and radiant skin

      The former host stands confidently in the snap, with her hands on her hips, looking into the distance dreamily. Her luscious brunette locks appear a little darker, and the TV star appears to have gone a little shorter, with a fair few inches cut off of her famous 'do, for an ultra-modern look. She has lined her eyes with smouldering dark kohl, and finished the party look with a pinky-toned nude lipstick.

      The dress has also been worn by fellow TV presenter Laura Whitmore

      The retro style platform heels are the perfect accessory for such a statement gown. The Morisey platforms by Dune London feature a front strap design, with an on-trend, chunky platform, and super high block heel. They come complete with an ankle buckle, and have a crocodile style print embedded on the velvet texture. Priced at £80, they are the ideal shoe for a festive gathering or Christmas party, and we are sure that renowned party girl Ms Flack will be rocking them all over December!
