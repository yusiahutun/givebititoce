    Meghann Fahy put her own spin on the red-carpet look while accepting her award for Breakout Style Star of the Year last night.

    The White Lotus star was honored at The Daily Front Row's seventh annual Fashion Los Angeles Awards held at The Beverly Hills Hotel. For the occasion, the actress unexpectedly paired a teeny black bikini top with black flared pants and a silky black suit jacket with an extra-long train which cascaded onto the red carpet.

    She finished the full Valentino look with abstract big silver earrings by Graziela Gems, glowy makeup, a pink glossy lip, and her signature beach waves.

    Michael Kovac//Getty Images

    Monica Schipper//Getty Images

    The Daily Front Row's award show is a star-studded event that honors stylists, fashion personalities, and style icons within Hollywood. Fahy had her breakout role in The Bold Type (2017) and has since become known for her sunny character, Daphne, in season two of The White Lotus. "I always saw her as a person," Fahy told Harper's Bazaar of Daphne last year. "And I always saw her as a pretty good person. I mean, I think that the more seriously you take the character, the more easily you can make that character feel [believable]. I think that the way that she presents her sunny disposition is one of her superpowers. Itâ€™s how she brings people into her and how she feels in control in social situations."

    Apart from acting, Fahy has collaborated with major fashion and beauty brands, including Armani Beauty and Ralph Lauren, whose form-fitting white dress she wore to the 2023 Screen Actors Guild Awards.

She has also written features for Rolling Stone, Teen Vogue, Forbes, and The Hollywood Reporter, among other outlets.
