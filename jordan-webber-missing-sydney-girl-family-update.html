    Jordan Webber Missing:  A 13-year-old Sydney girl, who went missing, has been located safe and well, bringing relief to her worried family and the community. To learn more about her family and case update, keep reading.

    The disappearance of 13-year-old Jordan Webber from Brighton Le Sands sent shockwaves through the community, prompting a frantic search effort by the New South Wales (NSW) police force and concerned citizens.

    However, in a positive turn of events, Jordan has been located safe and well. The NSW police force announced her discovery on Monday, May 29, 2023, providing much-needed relief to her worried family and friends.

    Jordan Webber Missing Case

    On the evening of Saturday, May 27, 2023, Webber was last seen at Brighton Le Sands, triggering immediate concern among her family and the community.

    As soon as her absence was noticed, the St George Police Area Command was alerted, and a thorough investigation was launched to determine her whereabouts.

    Subsequently, the police uncovered information suggesting that Jordan might have been in the Engadine area around 11:15 p.m. on Sunday, May 28, 2023.

    This discovery intensified the search efforts, as her family grew increasingly worried about her well-being.

    The news of her disappearance spread rapidly, with people across Sydney sharing information in hopes of locating her and ensuring her safe return.

    Fortunately, the search for Jordan came to a positive conclusion when she was found safe and well.

    The discovery brought immense relief not only to her family but also to the entire community that had been anxiously following the developments surrounding her disappearance.

    The efforts of the NSW police force, combined with the collective support and concern of the public, played a crucial role in ensuring Jordan’s safe recovery.

    Jordan Webber Age And Family-Sydney Girl

    As a 13-year-old girl, there were serious concerns for Jordan’s welfare, considering her young age.

    Described as having a Caucasian appearance, standing approximately 167cm tall, with a medium build and red hair, Jordan was last seen wearing a grey jacket, black track pants, and carrying a black backpack.

    She was known to frequent the Engadine and Miranda areas.

    During the period when Jordan was missing, the police and the community appealed for information to aid in her safe return. Authorities urged anyone with relevant information to contact the Kogarah Police or Crime Stoppers on 1800 333 000.

    Jordan Webber Case Update

    The NSW police force announced on their official Facebook page that Jordan had been located safe and well in Blacktown at approximately 1:30 a.m. on Friday, June 2, 2023.

    The authorities expressed their gratitude to the media and the community for their assistance during the search for Jordan.

    However, the situation took an unexpected turn when Jordan’s brother commented on the police’s Facebook post.

    He revealed that Jordan had deliberately gone into hiding on her own accord, explaining that she did not wish to return to foster care. He stated, “I’m her brother. She is not lost; she doesn’t want to return to foster care and is being a pain. I’ve contacted her, and she is in the Padstow region. But beyond that, she won’t tell me her location.”

    He further expressed frustration, saying, “It is just in her nature. She has been with lovely carers who have tried to help her a lot, and she has been ungrateful.”

    At present, there are no further details available regarding Jordan’s family or the circumstances surrounding her decision to go into hiding. Authorities have not released any recent updates on her situation.

    Rest assured, we will continue to monitor the case closely and provide any developments as they arise.

    The safe recovery of the 13-year-old has brought relief to her family, friends, and the wider community. The diligent efforts of the NSW police force, the media, and concerned citizens played a crucial role in ensuring her safe return.

    While the circumstances behind her decision to go into hiding remain unclear, it is hoped that Jordan will receive the support and care she needs during this challenging time.
