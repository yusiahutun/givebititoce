    A Korean singer has stunned thousands of fans after revealing her true age - and that of her much younger husband.

    Shim Mina, 51, is best known for her 'ageless' physique and myriad of dance videos she shares on social media.

    Following a recent TikTok trend, the singer posed with her husband and revealed their birth years while performing a dance.

    The now-viral video shows that Mina was born in 1972, while her husband, Ryu Philip, was born in 1989 - making him only 34 years old.

    The couple married in 2018 when Mina was 46 and Philip was 29.
