    The most expensive cars are fun to drive and perform better than economy cars. They often have the latest technology integrations, safety features, and performance components. Rarely will you spot these automobiles upcountry because their owners don't use them for long-distance journeys.

    List of the most expensive cars in Kenya and their prices

    Most rich folk in Kenya cruise their posh auto-machines within the country's cities and major towns. Onlookers are usually left gazing at the supermachines. Here is a list of the most expensive cars in Kenya and their prices:

    1. Ferrari Daytona SP3 2022 model – Kshs. 312 million

    A Ferrari is the most expensive car in Kenya at the moment. Several entrepreneurs in Kenya own these cars. A Ferrari Daytona SP3 2022 is among the latest models of the Ferrari Icona series. The car costs more than Kshs. 312 million. Daytona SP3 lives up to its billing. Given its weight-to-power ratio, it should have no trouble hitting 60 mph in less than three seconds. It's a pure hypercar with a pure-petrol, naturally aspirated V12 powertrain.

    2. Bugatti Veyron Grand Sport Vitesse model – Kshs. 300 million

    A Bugatti Veyron 16.4 Grand Sport Vitesse is among the fastest cars globally. It can hit up to 410km/h and reach a high speed of 0-100km/h in 2.6 seconds. News of a Bugatti Veyron in Kenya broke after the vehicle was spotted in Mombasa, Kenya's coastal city. A Bugatti Veyron Grand Sport Vitesse is worth $2,251,560 (over Kshs. 300 million). With that amount of money, you can get yourself a helicopter.

    3. Mercedes-Benz Pullman S600 model – Kshs. 140 million

    The vehicle is mainly reserved for politicians and dignitaries. It can run on a flat tire and has a twin-turbo V-12 engine. Kenya's former president, Uhuru Kenyatta, bought a new Mercedes-Benz Pullman S600 for Kshs. 100 million in 2019. This car has a 12,000-plus-pound curb weight, is 100% bulletproof, and is limited to a top speed of 99 mph. It costs around $1 million in the US (over Kshs. 140 million).

A Lamborghini Aventador 2021 model might not be the most expensive vehicle in Kenya, but it is no doubt one of the fastest cars in the country. A Lamborghini Aventador can cost as high as Ksh. 106 million.

    5. Rolls Royce Wraith Coupe 2023 model – Kshs. 91 million

    Rolls Royce Phantom is among the new cars in Kenya. Indian businessman Raju Sanghani owns one. The vehicle has been spotted on several occasions on Kenyan roads. The starting price of a new Rolls Royce Phantom in Kenya is Kshs. 40 million. A Rolls Royce Wraith Coupe 2023 model goes for Kshs. 91 million. The top speed of the Rolls Royce Phantom is 155 mph.

    10. Ferrari 458 Italia Convertible – Kshs. 37 million

    A Ferrari 458 Italia Convertible might not be the latest model of its series, but it is still pretty expensive. This car is a work of art and aerodynamic efficiency. It has a 4.5L naturally aspirated V8 engine, an acceleration of 0-100 km/h in 3.4 seconds, and a top speed of over 325 km/h (202 mph).

It has a top speed of 190 mph, a 6.0 liter W12 engine, and other impressive features.

    Why are cars expensive in Kenya?

    The price of cars in Kenya is high because the supply is lower than the demand. Since traders import more second-hand cars than new cars, purchasing a new car and the latest model is very costly.
