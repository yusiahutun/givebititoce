    DIY Friday: Craft These Custom Invitations for Your Next Party!

    Planning a party to celebrate Spring with family and friends? Craft these awesome custom invitations, courtesy of Home &amp; Family stars Debbie Matenopoulos and Paige Hemmis! Check out the DIY instructions below.

    05 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Planning a party to celebrate Spring with family and friends? Craft these awesome custom invitations, courtesy of ‘Home & Family’ stars Debbie Matenopoulos and Paige Hemmis! Check out the DIY instructions below.

    (Copyright 2015 Crown Media Family Networks/Photographer: Jeremy Lee)

    Use the following instructions to craft your own custom party invitations!

    Materials:

    • Order cards from a local store or online at Hallmark.com

    • Strings, buttons, stickers or additional materials to your choosing

    • Glue (if using buttons)

    • Order your own custom USB drive from CustomUSB.com

    • Boxes that will fit you invitation and USB drive of choice

    • Ribbons

    Directions:

  • Be sure to order a card that matches the theme/tone of your event

  • Add personal touch to the card with matching string, stickers, buttons or more. Get creative!

  • Upload a video message to your custom USB drive. Include videos, photos and a special message

  • Pack your invitation and USB drive into a box

  • Decorate the box with matching ribbons and strings

  • Hand deliver or ship your invitations and look forward to the responses as guests, family and friends receive their unique invitations!

    • Watch Debbie and Paige give a tutorial on how to craft the invitations in the following video!
