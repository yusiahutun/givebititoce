    Jedediah Hall’s body recovery

    Towards the end of April 2022, Adventure with Purpose and Profiling Evil contacted the Idaho Fall Police Department regarding Jed's search. The team had learned about his case from their viewers, former stories from the media, and IFPD releases on Hall’s disappearance.

    On the morning of 1st May 2022, police officers from IDFPD, the Adventure with Purpose team, and Jed's parents met at the Snake River Boat Launch near the 1400 block of River Parkway, ready to begin the search.

    The Adventures with a Purpose team immediately located the Nissan Versa approximately 75 yards downstream from the boat ramp. The vehicle was 50 feet from the shore, where the water was approximately 8 feet deep.
