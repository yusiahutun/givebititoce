    So what do we know about her?

    Karen Houghton chose a life away from the cameras

    Houghton chose a path far removed from the cameras, favouring a quieter life. Following a career as a flight attendant, Houghton then worked as a part-time nurse and also published two cookbooks, per British media.

    Karen Houghton and Kris Jenner’s complicated relationship

    Jenner and Houghton – the reality star’s only sister – had their ups and downs. After their parents split, they grew up in California with their mum, Mary Jo “MJ” Campbell. Jenner wrote about her and Houghton sticking together in her 2011 memoir, Kris Jenner … and All Things Kardashian: “We loved each other, and we were there for each other through thick and thin, and to this day, we are a part of each other’s lives.”But then things got rocky: Houghton stirred the pot at Kim Kardashian’s baby shower in 2013, and later threw some shade at Jenner in interviews, calling her “vain”.In a twist, Houghton eventually got a facelift in 2016, saying it made her feel closer to Jenner’s look, per British media. By 2019, it seemed the sisters patched things up, showing off a happy family photo.

    Karen Houghton is survived by her daughter, Natalie Zettel

    Houghton leaves behind a daughter, 26-year-old Natalie Zettel – her only child, born from her marriage to Mark Zettel – and Natalie’s fiancé, Noah Warren. The pair became engaged in July 2022.

    Zettel shared her heartache on Instagram with a series of photos, showing her close bond with her mum and the life they shared.

    “Dear Mommy, I can’t believe you’re gone,” Natalie wrote on Instagram. “You had the biggest heart. And I don’t know how to go on without you. It all happened so quick and I wasn’t ready for you to go.”

    The photos ranged from Natalie’s first days to fun Halloween costumes, showcasing a shared life full of joy, love and pets.

    According to her Instagram, Natalie is a model and entrepreneur. Her brand Styles by Nat is a clothing rental subscription service.
