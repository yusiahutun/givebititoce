    Kylie Jenner Reacts to Speculation She Changed Her Style for Timothe Chalamet

    22 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Any longtime follower of Kylie Jenner can tell you the mogul has undergone a quiet style metamorphosis over the past year. Gone are the days of King Kylie—enter Fashion Week Kylie, who makes regular viral front-row appearances at Jacquemus, Jean Paul Gaultier, and Schiaparelli runway shows (clad in a faux-lion-head gown, no less).

    In a new interview with The New York Times, Jenner confirmed what fans already knew: Her personal style started to shift about a year ago. Around that time, her son, Aire, turned one year old.

    “It takes me a while after I have a baby to feel like myself again,” she explained to the publication. “Then you get your body back, and you’re like, ‘Wait, the trends have changed.’”

    Some observers have speculated that Jenner’s sartorial transformation was due to her romance with Timothée Chalamet, as she also began dating the Dune star about a year ago. Asked about that theory, she responded coyly: “I don’t know how I feel about that.” She succinctly added, “I just don’t want to talk about personal things.”

    Both Jenner and Chalamet have kept a tight lid on their relationship since dating rumors first emerged in April 2023, though Chalamet did seem to have his love life in mind while commenting on the intense public scrutiny he receives in an interview with GQ last October.

    “You’ve got to have the experiences in your personal life that are usable to you,” he said at the time. “The experiential rush of my career taking off was so new to me that those were the experiences that were feeding my work for a while. But you’ve got to have real experiences. Human experiences. You’ve got to fall in love, you’ve got to be bored.”

    Christopher Polk

    Elsewhere in the Times interview, Jenner opened up about her responsibilities as a mother to Aire and his sister Stormi, six, both of whom she shares with ex-boyfriend Travis Scott.

    Writer Jessica Testa noted that Stormi entered the room where the interview was taking place multiple times throughout the talk, and that Jenner had subsequently asked for her daughter’s quotes to be omitted from the published article. “We can’t do that to her,” Jenner said. “She’s innocent. She comes in here, and she doesn’t know we’re doing an interview and then she has these quotes out in the open and she’s six years old. It’s my responsibility to protect her.”
