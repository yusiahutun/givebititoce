    Ana de la Reguera is a Mexican actress. She began acting in telenovelas during the mid-1990s. In the 2000s, she had roles in some Mexican films before landing a part in the Mexican-American feature film Nacho Libre. The actress became further known in the 2010s for her work in the US. She first starred as Vida in the HBO series Eastbound & Down. At the same time, she guest-starred in Royal Pains, Anger Management, and The Blacklist. In 2015, she played Elisa Alvaro in the Netflix series Narcos. More recently, she starred as Alicia Jimenez on the Starz series Power. She also played Marisol Silva on the Amazon series Goliath. In the movies, she had performances in Cop Out, Cowboys & Aliens, Jessabelle, and Everything, Everything.
