    13 Jun 2024, 00:00

    While the jury is still out on whether blondes really do have more fun, one thing is certain: blonde is the go-to shade for many stars. Selena Gomez, Beyoncé, and Taylor Swift are just a few of our favorite celebs who have dabbled in the sunny shade.

    With platinum, caramel, strawberry, and even bronde to choose from, blonde is an extremely versatile shade that works for just about anyone. So, if you're thinking of going blonde for the first time or looking to change up your current shade, you've come to the right place. Below are 30 blonde celebrities to inspire you.

    Meet the expert

    Bright, Buttery Blonde

    Gwyneth Paltrow is known for her signature unfussy blonde bends. Here, she wears them in various shades of bright, buttery blonde.

    Silver Blonde

    One of the most popular shades of blonde among fashion-forward celebs is silver platinum. Kim Kardashian wears this icy spin on super-long hair and pairs it with her natural dark roots for a nice contrast.

    Dirty Blonde

    A neutral blonde shade that mixes light and dark tones throughout, dirty blonde is one of the most versatile ways to wear blonde and can be customized to your personal preferences. Here, Ciara opts for a cool-toned dirty blonde with bright blonde sitting on top of a darker base.

    Golden Bronde

    As colorist and R+Co collective member Richy Kandasamy once told InStyle, bronde is a combination of light-to-medium blonde mixed with brunette colors. "The result is a sun-kissed appearance with a natural blend of lighter and darker shades, creating vibrant yet subtle movement within the hair," he said. We love Beyoncé's caramel blonde highlights for their warm, glowing effect.

    Bright Blonde Highlights

    If you don't want to commit to a full-coverage shade, opt for highlights. Aniston brightens her dark base with bright blonde highlights that bring added depth to her layers.

    Shadow Roots

    Shadow roots (also known as grown-out roots) are everywhere lately and make for a more edgy and dynamic hair look. Here, Dua Lipa lets her natural dark shade peek out under her long, bleached-out hair.

    Bleached Blonde With Shadow Roots

    While we love Selena Gomez's signature dark brunette hair, we have a soft spot for her bleached blonde moment with shadow roots. Paired with the ever-popular lob cut, it's one of our favorite looks ever.

    Strawberry Blonde Ombré

    You can't go wrong with a gradient blonde that transitions naturally from the root to your ends. Serena Williams wears a strawberry-toned blonde ombré in loose, long waves for a stunning look.

    Bombshell Blonde

    Sydney Sweeney channels Old Hollywood glamour with her bright blonde shade punctuated by darkened undertones. It's simple, classic, and chic.

    Icy Blonde

    An icy blonde shade that's as bright as snow looks beautiful on Anya Taylor-Joy. Her long Rapunzel lengths add a regal vibe, too.

    Two-Toned Blonde

    This is another instance that showcases the beauty of dark roots for blondes. Gigi Hadid's cool-toned base transitions beautifully into her pale golden blonde.

    Honey Blonde

    Opt for a honey blonde shade if you want something warm and cozy. Rosie Huntington-Whiteley mixes a soft, dark blonde base with creamy blonde highlights seamlessly woven through.

    Warm Ombré

    If warmer undertones work better for you, opt for a sun-kissed ombré. Margot Robbie's dark roots slowly transform into a golden blonde for a soft, classic look.

    Platinum Afro

    Make a striking hair statement and wear your natural curls out in a golden platinum Afro. Solange looks nothing short of angelic.

    Two-Toned Lowlights

    It doesn't get more rock 'n' roll than wearing two contrasting shades. Miley Cyrus opts for a jet-black base with white platinum blonde for the ultimate cool-girl look.

    Shimmering Golden Ombré

    Jennifer Lopez's blonde looks are always top-tier. We especially love it when she goes bright and golden with her blonde ombré.

    Neutral Blonde Ombré

    You don't need much to elevate a wavy lob. But as Jessica Alba illustrates, a neutral blonde-powered ombré adds even more visual interest.

    Creamy Blonde

    Reese Witherspoon's creamy blonde with medium highlights is soft, diffused, and totally classic.

    Strawberry Base

    If you want a touch of ginger for your blonde shade, opt for a strawberry blonde like Nicole Kidman's.

    Pink Tint

    Blonde comes in fantasy tones, too. Blackpink's Rosé is often seen with pink hair, and here she rocks platinum strands with a subtle rosy tint.

    Platinum Blonde Buzz Cut

    We love a buzz cut and Cynthia Erivo wears hers in a silvery platinum blonde that's extra fierce.

    Deep Gold

    Can blonde hair be dark and moody? Leave it to Taylor Swift to prove that it can be with the right updo. Here, she wears her signature blonde in a deeper bronde tone.

    Babylights Balayage

    You know to turn to balayage for low-maintenance highlights that add beautiful dimension. Sarah Jessica Parker wears bright blonde babylight for lived-in, natural-looking color.

    Cool Bronde

    Showing off the beauty of cool-toned hair, Laverne Cox wears a mix of ash-blonde highlights to add contrast to her deeper bronde base.

    Blonde Tips

    Here, Jamie Chung's dark chocolate lengths gradually transition to bright golden blonde. Opt for lighter tips like her's when you want that extra pop.

    Beachy Blonde

    Sticking to her Southern Californian roots (pun intended), Khloé Kardashian goes beach blonde with her long, wavy hair. It's bright and sun-kissed effect makes us yearn for warmer beach days.

    Ice Blonde Highlights

    If you're a fan of cooler blonde tones, then opt for a shimmering pale blonde. Gisele Bündchen's icy highlights add a certain luministory.

    Ice Queen Blonde

    For another edgier option, Cara Delevigne treads into fantasy territory with her icy platinum blonde.

    Golden Bronde

    Hair idol Blake Lively brightens up her deep bronde base with golden blonde highlights for a striking contrast that's just mesmerizing.

    Caramel Blonde Highlights

    Chrissy Teigen's glowing caramel blonde highlights add depth to her neutral base in an elegant and ultra-flattering way.
