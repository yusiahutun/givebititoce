    Bruno Mars Adds Historic 3rd Hawaii Show Following 2 Sold-Out Dates At Aloha Stadium

    .inarticle_banner_top { width: 728px; height: 90px; } @media(max-width: 800px) { .inarticle_banner_top { width: 300px; height: 250px; } } @media(min-width: 800px) { .inarticle_banner_top { width: 728px; height: 90px; } }

    15 Jun 2024, 00:00

    HONOLULU, HI (CelebrityAccess) — Following two sold-out dates, and unprecedented demand, Bruno Mars has added a 3rd Hawaii show to his 24k Magic World Tour. Produced by Live Nation, the newly announced show will take place Thursday, November 8 at Aloha Stadium in Honolulu.

    The trio of homecoming performances is record-breaking for the stadium, with no other artist in its history booking that number of dates in succession.

    The 135 date 24K Magic World Tour is Mars’ first full-length outing since his hugely successful Moonshine Jungle World Tour in 2013 and was even credited with selling 1 million tickets in a single day.

    Tickets are available to the general public starting Friday, August 3rd at 10 am HST via Ticketmaster.com. The sales will be zip code restricted to Hawaii residents for the first 48 hours.
