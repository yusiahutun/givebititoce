    Table of Biography

    Austin Butler is a popular American actor. Austin Butler began his career on the Disney Channel and Nickelodeon and later on in teen dramas.

    Early Life and Childhood

    Austin Butler was born on August 17, 1991, in the city of Anaheim, California, United States. His age is 31 years as of 2023 and his full name is Austin Robert Butler.

    Similarly, he is American by nationality and Christian by religion. Moreover, his zodiac sign is Leo which belongs to the element of Fire, according to astrologers.

    He was born to David Butler (father) and an aesthetician Lori Butler (mother). Likewise, he has one sister named Ashley Butler and she is a background actor by profession.

    Education

    In terms of his educational background, he has done his schooling at a Community school up to seventh grade in Anaheim. Later, he did home-school to accommodate his work schedule and he took the California High School Proficiency Exam (CHSPE) to finish his formal high school education.

    Career and Professional Life

    Moving on to his career, he started his acting career on television, first in roles on the Disney Channel and Nickelodeon and later on in teen dramas.

    In 2005, after working as an extra on various television series, he was featured as a background actor playing the role of Zippy Brewster for two seasons on Nickelodeon’s Ned’s Declassified School Survival Guide.

    Moreover, in May 2007, he appeared in a guest-starring role on the Disney Channel series Hannah Montana playing the role of Derek Hanson. Furthermore, he played the main role of James Garrett on the Nickelodeon series, Zoey 101, in February 2008.

    Similarly, he appeared as Jake Pearson in 20th Century Fox’s family adventure film Aliens in the Attic in July 2009. Likewise, he also played Jones on the CW series Life Unexpected in February 2010. In 2011, he was cast as Wilke in the ABC Family series Switched at Birth, which premiered on June 27, 2011.

    Also, he was cast as Sebastian Kydd in The CW’s Sex and the City prequel series The Carrie Diaries in March 2012. Similarly, in the 2015 thriller film The Intruders, he co-starred with Miranda Cosgrove and Tom Sizemore. In 2016, he debuted as Wil Ohmsford in The Shannara Chronicles.

    Moreover, in 2019, he played a fictionalized version of Manson Family member Tex Watson in the Quentin Tarantino film Once Upon a Time in Hollywood. In addition, he was cast as Elvis Presley in Elvis, directed by Baz Luhrmann.

    The film had its world premiere at the 2022 Cannes Film Festival, where it received a twelve-minute standing ovation from the audience. Recently, in 2022 he hosted an episode of Saturday Night Live. Also, in 2023, he will star in the war drama miniseries Masters of the Air.
