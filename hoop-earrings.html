    Jennifer Fisher

    Jennifer Fisher

    Everyone needs a simple pair of slim hoops in their repertoire. Jennifer Fisher is a go-to for staples like this 14 karat gold-vermeil design – they're so lightweight you can wear them all day long.

    Tiffany

    Originally designed by Elsa Peretti, these Tiffany earrings are made from sterling silver and suspended by two diamond studs.

    Piaget

    Made from 18-karat rose gold, Piaget's open hoops are suspended from a statement stud dotted with 20 white diamonds. These would make a very special gift, if you want to think beyond the basic pendant necklace.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    YSSO

    All of YSSO's designs are inspired by Greek culture and mythology: these Fire hoops are inspired by Prometheus, the God of fire. Made from bronze double-plated in gold, these hoops have a beautiful abstract shape – and, since they're hand-carved, no two pairs are the same.

    Monica Vinader X Kate Young

    Monica Vinader X Kate Young

    From Monica Vinader's second collection with Kate Young, these gold hoops are one of those hero jewellery items that will effortlessly elevate everything in your wardrobe. We love the chamfered shape.

    Cartier

    Cartier's iconic Love bangle now comes in hoop earring form. These huggies are cast from 18-karat yellow gold and engraved with those instantly recognisable lock symbols.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    Otiumberg

    Otiumberg's double drop earrings are convertible, so you can two looks for the price of one: simply detach the gold hoop to wear the crystal-embellished pair solo.

    Daphine

    Beloved London-based jewellery brand Daphine has now landed on Matches Fashion. We love the ridged effect on these gold-vermeil hoop earrings.

    Mateo NY

    Mateo NY is the fine jewellery brand every woman needs on her radar. There are plenty of unique hoop earring to choose from in the designer's most recent collection, but this convertible pair feels really special. Crafted from 14-karat gold, they're studded with 1.30 carats of sparkling diamonds and drop to a sizeable freshwater pearl that can be detached to create a subtler daytime look.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    Missoma

    From Missoma's serpent collection, these earrings are defined by their woven 'snakeskin' texture that catches the light so beautifully. Choose between retro yellow-gold or a cooler silver tone.

    Edge of Ember

    Edge of Ember

    From Edge of Ember's Solstice collection, these hoop earrings have a beautiful, organic feel. It's all down the hammered gold-plated sterling silver and the faceted white topaz and black spinel gemstones.

    Amina Muaddi

    Amina Muaddi

    Now 30% Off

    Amina Muaddi stormed with industry with her bejewelled footwear, but did you know the designer does jewellery? These hoop earrings are studded with crystals of differing sizes to really catch the light.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    Kiki McDonough

    Kiki McDonough

    Kiki McDonough's convertible hoops are a lifelong investment. Made from 18-carat yellow gold, they're studded with bezel diamonds and drop to a stunning square-cut amethyst. These earrings are ideal for formal evening events, but you can detach the sparkling pendant for a low-key look during the day.

    Van Cleef & Arpels

    Van Cleef & Arpels

    Cast from 18-karat rose gold, Van Cleef & Arpels ornate beaded hoop earrings are finished with 0.62 carats of diamonds set in the shape of the brand's signature clover motif.

    Pandora

    Pandora is switching things up at the moment and we like what we see: there are plenty more gold options, and the brand's signature silver pieces are being elevated with freshwater pearls and charms. Wear these logo-stamped huggies alone or choose from a plethora of pendants from the new collection.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    Boucheron

    Part of the Quatre lineup, Boucheron's Radiant Edition pieces focus on the contemporary beauty of graphic lines. These hoops earrings are crafted from both 18-karat yellow and white gold, with the former fluted and the latter dusted in diamonds to create dramatic contrast.

    Monica Vinader

    Monica Vinader

    If you're a fan of second, even third, piercings, you need to be armed with a collection of huggies. Monica Vinader's are hammered for texture, so they look great solo; but they also can be styled with the brands tiny ear charms, too.

    De Beers

    De Beers' hoop earrings are made from 18-karat white gold encircled with sparkling micropavé diamonds.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    Giovanni Raspini

    Giovanni Raspini

    Bamboo hoops have been rooted in Hip Hop culture since the Eighties, and the style was in the spotlight once again when Carrie Bradshaw made them part of her uniform in Sex and the City. Giovanni Raspini's are made sterling silver that's dipped in bright, yellow gold.

    By Alona

    We love the hammered effect on By Alona's Panarea hoops. This design comes in several sizes so you can layer to perfection.
