    Early Life and Childhood

    Stewart comes from a true show business family. She is the daughter of multi-hyphenate, legendary stuntman-actor-producer-and more Nils and part-time actress Renee Steward. Moreover, she is called by the name “Fivel” simply because she likes it better than her given birth name, which is Trent.

    Similarly, her mother works as a part-time actress whereas her father was a professional stuntman and has showcased his talents in over 100 movies. The young actress has mixed ethnicity. Therefore, she has Scottish, Russian, and Blackfoot Native American descent/ancestry from her father’s side while her mother is of Asian descent, more precisely of Chinese, Japanese, and Korean descent.

She has even starred as Chrissy Deavers in the romantic comedy ‘Yard Sale’ in the same year.
