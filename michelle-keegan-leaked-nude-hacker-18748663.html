    Michelle Keegan is one of the British stars who appears on the "master list" of nude pictures alleged to have been obtained by a hacker.

    The former Corrie star is included on a list of over 100 female stars which was published to a 4chan thread last night by an unknown hacker.

    A representative for Keegan told the Daily Star Online: "There is no evidence that Michelle has been hacked."

    However, the pictures were reportedly obtained from an Apple iCloud leak, meaning the hack may not have been noticed by any of the targets.

    Michelle isn't the only Brit babe that has been featured on the list.
