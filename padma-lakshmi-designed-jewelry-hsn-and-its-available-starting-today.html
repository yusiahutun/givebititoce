    Padma Lakshmi is a cookbook author, an actress, and now, a jewelry designer, too—and her first collection of PL by Padma Lakshmi trinkets debuts today on HSN. The multi-talented star, who also distributes her culinary line, Easy Exotic, though HSN, will be on the air during the channel's Spring Fashion event to present her boho-inspired gold pieces, from bead-accented chandelier earrings to a laser-cut leopard-print ring, all priced between $16 and $90. "My designs are most notable for bridging the gap between East and West," Lakshmi told InStyle.com. "I love the rich heritage and beauty of traditional Indian jewelry, but I wanted to do a line that was more modern and streamlined and sleek without losing the femininity and ethnic influences that run through all my designs." And she accomplished that, with a range of statement-making pieces (a bold collar necklace) to delicate additions (a pave ring). "I wanted to create something that women would love to wear every day—pieces that work with jeans and a sweater as well as cocktail dresses and special occasions—jewelry that highlights the various sides of a woman's personality without upstaging her." Check your local TV listings to find out when Lakshmi will be presenting her collection to you for the first time, and see a selection of pieces on hsn.com now.
