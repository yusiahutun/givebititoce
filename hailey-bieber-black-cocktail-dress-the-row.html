    Hailey Bieber Paired Her Sleek Cocktail Dress With an Unexpected Accessory

    Fans of Hailey Biebers street style should be well acquainted with the models affinity for sleek and streamlined date night looks. But shes also not averse to occasionally remixing her typical uniform with an unexpected touch.

    27 May 2024, 00:00

    Fans of Hailey Bieber’s street style should be well acquainted with the model’s affinity for sleek and streamlined date night looks. But she’s also not averse to occasionally remixing her typical uniform with an unexpected touch.

    Nita Dress in Wool and Silk

    Nita Dress in Wool and Silk

    Case in point: The Rhode founder and her husband, Justin Bieber, headed out for dinner at celebrity hotspot Giorgio Baldi on Tuesday night. For the outing, Hailey made an elegant appearance in a strapless black midi dress with a body-hugging silhouette, selected from the Row. She enhanced the look with some of her go-to, sophisticated accessories: a glossy black shoulder bag, gold drop earrings from Bottega Veneta, and a thin gold chain necklace.

    Hailey finished off the look with a surprising addition: a glittering silver anklet, which sparkled in contrast with her classic, no-fuss black pumps. Anklets are having something of a renaissance this season, and the model just proved how versatile the trendy accessory really is.

    BACKGRID

    Slinky bodycon silhouettes are often Hailey’s go-to when it comes to dressing for a night out.

    For instance, she dined at Malibu’s Nobu last week while wearing a light gray jersey minidress from Venroy, which featured a draping detail at the waist and ruching down the sides. She paired the dress with a long, creamy trench coat, and white croc-embossed heels from Femme LA.

    Prior to that, she shared photos on Instagram of yet another summery date night ensemble. Then, she posed in a strapless Maygel Coronel dress in a shimmery tan, featuring more ruching details across the center and multiple cutouts at the abdomen. To accessorize, she wore gold earrings and a raffia purse, both from Saint Laurent.
