    Tedeschi Trucks Band Signs With MSG For Multi-City, Multi-Year Residency

    18 Mar 2024, 00:00

    NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess) — The Madison Square Garden Company announced that as part of the company’s first ever dual-city, multi-year booking agreement, the iconic jam favorites Tedeschi Trucks Band has signed on for a multi-year residency at both the Beacon Theatre in New York City and The Chicago Theatre in Chicago, IL.

    As part of the dual-city residency which extends through 2022, the band will play six shows or more each year at The Beacon beginning with their current run, which kicked off on October 5 and runs through October 13th. The band will also play three shows or more per year at The Chicago Theatre, beginning with four shows on January 18, 19, 25 and 26, 2019.

    Specific dates for performances beyond the October 2018 New York and January 2019 Chicago shows will be announced at a later date, as will on-sale timing for the 2019 Chicago shows, MSG said.

    The band’s 2018 New York and 2019 Chicago dates will mark the beginning of the residency and will be Tedeschi Trucks Band’s eighth consecutive run at The Beacon and fourth consecutive run at The Chicago Theatre.

    Both the Beacon Theatre and The Chicago Theatre have hosted Tedeschi Trucks Band multiple times since they first played each of the venues in 2011, with the band having played 31 shows at the Beacon Theatre and nine shows at The Chicago Theatre to date.

    The band’s autumn performances at the Beacon Theatre have become something of a tradition for the band’s fans, and both fans from the region and afield make the trek to attend the performances. All of the current spate of shows at the Beacon are currently fully sold-out. On-sale information for the January 2019 Chicago Theatre shows will be announced at a later date.

    Tedeschi Trucks Band is represented by booking agent Wayne Forte (Entourage Talent Associates, LTD) and managed by Blake Budney (Milestone Music Management).
