    Rob Gronkowski had a spontaneous dance-off with Travis Kelce at a beach party, and the clip hilariously shows one of the tight ends came out on top.

    The NFL legend, Rob ‘Gronk’ Gronkowski, shared the video with a talk show last week when he went on the show to discuss the upcoming Super Bowl, which Taylor Swift may not be able to attend. He praised the Kansas City Chiefs’ defense and is excited about the big game in Las Vegas coming in less than two weeks. On Monday, he told The Tonight Show, Starring Jimmy Fallon, that Travis Kelce was the perfect dance partner.

    Travis Kelce and Rob Gronkowski battled it out in a dance off

    In the clip, we can see the 34-year-old tight end, who announced his relationship with Taylor Swift last September, smoothly move onto a small stage to face Gronk.

    But it was tough to see who really won based on both of their impressive dance skills. In the clip, we see Fronk and Kelce walk towards each other, swaying their hips and holding their arms out in the air. Another video showed the tight ends shuffling like penguins.

    After, Rob Gronkowski tells Jimmy: “It’s kind of a tie”, but the host disagreed, telling him he totally won over Travis Kelce. Awkward.

    The impromptu competition took place at the 2022 Gronk Beach Party, which he hosts every year.

    The former NFL star added: “I mean, we’re great dance partners. Imagine if we were teammates on the field, on the same team as well.

    “Oh man, that would be pretty impressive. We would dominate like the dancer floor”.

    The Gronk beach Party is back

    Rob Gronkowski says his beach party is back, at Las Vegas on February 10, but it seems Travis Kelce won’t be attending this time because the Super Bowl is the next day. We can imagine Trav will need a good night’s rest, so we doubt he’ll be able to swing by!

    Trav will join his pal and quarterback Patrick Mahomes as they go up against the 49ers after defeating the Baltimore Ravens in the AFC Championship on Sunday.

    After the big defeat, Taylor rushed onto the field and embraced him, where the duo shared a kiss.

    But Rob Gronkowski couldn’t help but praise Travis Kelce and Mahomes with their recent stats.

    “Travis and Patrick have just been putting on a display for the NFL week in and week out,” he said. “There’s no doubt about that, but there’s one record that I am proud to hold to this day still, and it’s the single season receiving record for most touchdowns by a tight end.”

    “So if he breaks that one, I will be really bummed. But he has broken about every other record so far,” the retired NFL star added.
