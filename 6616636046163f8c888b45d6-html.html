    Larsa Pippen shares bikini snap after Marcus Jordan split

    05 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Larsa Pippen has treated her followers to a new bikini picture soon after the split from partner Marcus Jordan.

    She posted the image to her Instagram page on Tuesday and it seems she has been in the gym to help get through the breakup.

    With the caption, she suggested that she is only going to get more toned as the months go by, saying: "Gearing up for the summer".

    It has only been a few weeks since the daughter of Scottie Pippen split up with the son of Michael Jordan.

    They started going out in September 2022 and seemed to have an ideal relationship that many people were jealous of, and a wedding appeared to be on the way.

    The winter proved to be tough for the couple though and they went on a break, which turned into an official end to the relationship.

    Pippen wants her own path

    Jordan has been keeping fairly quiet on social media since they split up, but Pippen has been sharing pictures of herself and is putting up a very positive image.

    A source recently spoke about Pippen's desire to start a new path for herself.

    "Larsa is now focused on her exciting next chapter," he said. "She has always been someone who is ambitious and driven. She needs a partner who has similar qualities."

    But Marcus hit back at Larsa for "rewriting history" after their second breakup, and Michael Jordan's son appeared to criticise her for seeking "clout" by telling podcast hosts Amy Robach and TJ Holmes that Marcus wasn't the one.

    "I wasn't born last night. Rewriting history for clout is not cute," Marcus, 33, wrote in a since-deleted Instagram story. "Funny how ppl change, just to be part of a conversation that's irrelevant. Why give shorty a heart, when she'd rather have press? Ooooohhhhh, that's that nerve lol."
