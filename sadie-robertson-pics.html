    Sadie Robertson doesn’t have a beard, but she’s just as much part of Duck Dynasty as her hirsute family. Sadie – the granddaughter of Duck Commander founder Phil Robertson and the daughter of Korie and Willie Robertson – appeared on the A&E reality television show from 2012 to 2017. During that time, she also competed on one season of Dancing With The Stars, finishing second in 2014. Sadie also displayed some singing talent on Duck The Halls: A Robertson Family Christmas, as she sang “Away In A Manger” with Alison Krauss. A multi-talented performer, Sadie pursued acting, as she landed roles in faith-based films God’s Not Dead 2 and I’m Not Ashamed. In addition to all that, she’s also a bestselling writer, as she has written inspirational books like Live Original, Live Fearless, and Life Just Got Real: A Live Original Novel. With such a busy schedule, Sadie has also found love, as she and boyfriend Christian Huff became engaged on June 9, 2019.
