    Harry Styles’ mom has clapped back at body shamers in the best way, and fans are here for it.

    The music star’s mother posted a photo dump of her busy July month, and while thousands of fans were complimenting Anne in the comments, some weren’t so kind. However, she had the most ‘fantastic response’ which has fans hailing her as a ‘queen’.

    Harry Styles’ mom ‘claps back’ at body shamers

    Taking to her 2.7 million Instagram followers, Styles’ mom, Anne Twist posted a July photo dump, and as per her caption, included:

    “A wedding, a new baby, another hen do, trying not to fall off the back of a speeding boat, Italy with family & friends, limoncello, a detox, and another new baby.”

    Thousands of fans rushed to the comments to compliment Anne on how good she looked as one wrote: “That green dress on you Anne!” adding heart eyes and a fire emoji.

    One follower then directly replied to that comment saying: “That can’t be a recent pic because she looked very heavy in Italy.”

    Anne saw this comment and calmly replied by saying: “It’s a July pic. I’m sorry you feel the need to comment on my weight!” adding a ‘shrugging shoulders’ emoji.

    Fans were absolutely here for the sass as one responded: “Fantastic response. State the facts and move on!”

    “Anne always slays we love,” penned another.

    Harry’s mom says he ‘hasn’t changed’ since stardom

    Earlier this year, the Watermelon Sugar singer’s mom gave fans a rare insight into Harry’s life outside of fame in an interview with the Daily Mail.

    “He’s just the same as he has always been. As a very little boy, he was very much like he is now, just a smaller version,” she revealed.

    She then said she was “very proud” of Harry and his sister Gemma and “the adults they’ve become.”

    Liam Payne says he ‘misses’ the One Direction boys

    Although there STILL hasn’t been any news of a One Direction reunion, we’re still holding on to hope, and recently Liam fueled our delusions, even more, when he said he missed his bandmates in an adorable post.

    Taking to his 26 million Instagram followers, the 29-year-old posted a cute throwback photo of the boys, as he celebrated their debut single What Makes Your Beautiful almost reaching 1 billion streams on Spotify.

    Concluding the lengthy caption, he wrote: “I’m so happy that for 5 years we made such a great soundtrack to life for myself and everyone who got to listen sharing laughter pain, and everything in between miss you boys. Lots of love me x”

    We’re never giving up hope.
