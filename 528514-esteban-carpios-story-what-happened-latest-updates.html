    Esteban Carpio is an American prisoner born on 30 July 1978 in Boston, Massachusetts, United States. His mother is Yvonne Carpio. She is a former teacher at Hennigan Elementary School in Jamaica Plain.

    Esteban worked as a barber before he was sent to jail for the first-degree murder of a police officer. In 2005, Carpio dated Samein Phin, with whom he had a daughter. Samein is a former hustler.

    Esteban Carpio's story

    Esteban Carpio was arrested on 17 April 2005 and later charged with first-degree murder of a police officer named Detective Sgt. James Allen. While the Providence police officers were questioning Carpio for the stabbing of Madeline Gatta, an 84-year-old woman, a detective left the third-floor interview room to acquire water for Carpio.

    He left Allen with Carpio. Shortly after, a struggle ensued between Esteban and James Allen. Carpio grabbed Allen's gun and shot him twice in the chest, killing the 50-year-old officer on the spot. He then attempted to elude police by jumping out of a third-floor window but was captured 45 minutes later.
