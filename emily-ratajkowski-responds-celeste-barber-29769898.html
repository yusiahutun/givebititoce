    Emily Ratajkowski says "drama" with comedian Celeste Barber over a viral bikini photo poking fun at the model was "blown out of proportion".

    The fashion model was featured more than once in the Aussie mum's Instagram feed as part of her "Celeste Challenge Accepted" comedy stunt, where she recreates famous photos in increasingly-hilarious ways.

    One photo showed Celeste covering her breasts with her hand, mocking Emily's iconic nude photo, while another shows them in matching bikinis on a balcony.

    She said: "This whole drama with Celeste has been blown out of proportion, in general I find her to be quite funny.

    "But what I was trying to send the message to her was — I just don't want you to do this to me anymore.

    "I was like, I'm not giving you consent for this joke anymore."

    Emily explained that it happened at the time when she was promoting her swimwear and that she had posted a lot of pictures to promote it on Instagram.

    "I agree that the first time I saw Celeste, her Instagram, I thought it's hilarious. You have to be able to laugh at yourself, obviously, I think she's making fun of herself just as much as she's making fun of other people."

    She said: "I don't think Emily is a fan. That’s okay, she’s allowed to not love it. But she blocked me."
