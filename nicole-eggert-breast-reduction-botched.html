    Nicole Eggert's new pair is just right.

    The "Baywatch" alum is the latest to go under the knife on E!'s reality show "Botched" -- getting her large breast implants reduced to a more manageable size.

    The actress has had five boob jobs in the past and was only happy with one of them ... until now.

    On last night's episode, we finally saw the Before & After of Dr. Terry DuBrow's work, and it's clear that Eggert is loving her new look.

    "I'm really happy with the size," Nicole admitted in a post-op confessional. "Before my boobs were so big that it made everything look so big. It just was a downward spiral with those fake boobs, they weren't helping me with my weight loss goals at all. Now, I can work with it. They are so much more fitting for my body type!"

    The actress has been struggling with weight loss, but hopes to reach her goals now that she has smaller breasts.

    "This procedure has definitely propelled me in the right direction to get where I need to be," she said. "Before, I was having trouble losing weight because of the big boobs, I felt like I couldn't see any difference and it was just discouraging. Now I feel like being healthy is one step closer."

    "I already look 10 pounds lighter. I feel like I have sexy boobs now, I just have to get the rest of me going with it. It's a start," she added.
