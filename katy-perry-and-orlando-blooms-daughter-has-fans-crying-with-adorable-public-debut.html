    Katy Perry and Orlando Bloom’s three-year-old daughter made her first public appearance this weekend, at her mom’s Saturday night Las Vegas residency Play.

    The music star welcomed her first daughter with Hollywood star Orlando Bloom back in 2020, and now, just like her mom, she made her first public appearance in front of a huge crowd.

    Katy Perry’s daughter Daisy makes first public appearance

    Daisy Dove was shining like a superstar this week, as she made her first public appearance on stage with her mother.

    In the adorable moment, posted on X, formerly known as Twitter, the star was gearing up to perform her hit single, Hot and Cold when she shouted out her daughter in the crowd.

    As the camera panned to the adorable toddler, the singer could be heard saying: “Daisy! I love you so much. You’re my best friend, I’m so glad you’re here!”

    “I’m gonna sing this next song, I think you know it, okay?” Perry told her daughter.

    She then began to explain how “it was in 2008, and mommy was a little bit wild back then.”

    Her movie star partner, Orlando Bloom was also in the crowd, as the 39-year-old tributed him for being “an amazing father.”

    In a separate clip on X, Orlando could be seen dancing along to his partner’s song “Chained to the Rhythm” with his daughter.

    “Shes so adorable,” penned one fan.

    Another wrote: “I am crying she is the cutest.”

    Katy Perry and Orlando Bloom’s relationship explored

    The celebrity couple first met in 2016, at a Golden Globes after party, and got engaged three years later, in 2019.

    Perry was previously married to British comedian Russell Brand for 14 months, from October 2010, to December 2011.

    The Pirates of The Caribbean star popped the question on Valentine’s Day 2019, as he hired a helicopter to land on a roof top in Los Angeles, with her whole family there.

    The couple welcomed their baby girl Daisy, in 2020. As of yet, they’re still not married, although sources say they were set to wed in Japan, which was postponed due to the pandemic.
