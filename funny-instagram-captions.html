    There's a lot to be said for keeping it light-hearted and lively on Instagram. Life gets serious enough, after all—as does Facebook, what with the constant political brawling, and let's not even get started on the total three-ring circus Twitter has turned into lately. Pleasing pics with funny Instagram captions provide an uplifting alternative to the negative noise that can fill social media, but it can be darn difficult to come up with good jokes on the spot. Let's be honest...it's often a bit easier to brainstorm more straightforward or heartfelt IG captions around the seasons, vacations, and big life events like weddings, graduation and prom, than it is ones that make your followers LOL.

    That's why we've stepped up to help you get your hilarious on with 75 captions that are so funny you just might become the new queen of the Insta scene. We have captions to go along with your most glam (or not) selfies, shots of you and your squad, couple images, food photos, and even birthday snaps. Jot 'em down, bookmark this page, or, better yet, keep it open on your phone and at the ready. The next time you post you'll be sure to leave 'em laughing—and coming back for more!
