    Meghan Markle wedding SHOES: Bride opts for white satin Givenchy-style stilettos

    24 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Meghan Markle wore a long-sleeved Givenchy wedding dress by British designer Clare Waight Keller as she wed Prince Harry  today.

    The 36-year-old stunned in the bespoke fitted gown, which she teamed with a show-stopping 16ft veil.

    The brunette beauty also opted for the Queen Mary Diamond Bandeau tiara for her big day.

    But what about her wedding shoes?

    Royal Wedding LIVE: Harry and Meg begin carriage procession View gallery

    Meghan opted for classic white court shoes for her big day. The pointed shoes feature rather large stiletto heels and appear to be made from a similar white material to her stunning wedding dress.

    According to Kensington Palace, the wedding shoes are based on a Givenchy refined pointed couture design made of a silk duchess satin.

    It is currently unclear who actually designed her wedding shoes.

    Every detail of Meghan’s outfit had meaning. Her veil, made from silk tulle, featured a trim of hand-embroidered flowers from each of the 53 counties in the Commonwealth.

    While the bride followed royal protocol and paid homage to her new family by wearing a tiara straight from the Queen’s jewelry vault.
