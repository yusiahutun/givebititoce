    American reality star Kate Gosselin has undergone a secret cosmetic procedure to enhance her breasts, according to new claims.

    The Jon & Kate Plus 8 TV personality, 35, is said to have had the surgery early last year, around the time of her separation from Jon Gosselin, her husband of ten years.

    An insider told Us Weekly magazine that Miss Gosselin – who was a contestant on this year’s season of Dancing with the Stars – was advised on the surgery by her close confidant and personal bodyguard Steve Neild.

    Sizing up: Kate Gosselin looked very busty while filming her new show Kate and 8 last month, adding fuel to rumours that she's had a boob job

    Less buxom: Kate was photographed at a TLC event in March of 2008

    ‘Kate was going to have one kind of breast augmentation, but she changed her mind after talking to Steve about what would look best,’ an insider was quoted as saying.

    Insiders revealed that Neild – a married father-of-two – drove his famous client to the doctor’s office when it allegedly took place on March 30, 2009.

    The magazine went on the claim that ‘multiple sources’ confirmed that the blonde star had definitely had the procedure.

    The revelations will come as a surprise to fans, since Miss Gosselin claimed she was opposed to the idea of surgery when asked about the subject earlier this year.

    ‘Plastic surgery? Please. Who has time to even think about it, let alone do it?,’ she was quoted as saying.

    However, Us Weekly pointed out that a figure-reducing tummy tuck was featured on an episode of her hit TLC show in 2007.

    The belly-flattening procedure was reportedly performed by the father of Hailey Glassman, who then famously went on to date her womanising husband.

    Miss Gosselin has now gone on to launch a new show, Kate Plus Eight, which focuses on her life as a single mother.

    She is also launching another reality show later this summer, ‘Twist Of Kate’, which will show her taking various new jobs in America.
