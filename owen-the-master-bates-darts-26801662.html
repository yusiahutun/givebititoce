    From The Power to The Iceman and Snakebite – everyone loves a good darts nickname.

    Being recognised by your alias is almost as important as your ability to nail treble 20s on a regular basis. For up-and-coming players, it’s a key part of getting your name on the darting map. One young player has certainly managed that, if not perhaps for the reasons he intended.

    Loxley Darts has recently snapped up Owen Bates as one of its representatives. In announcing the good news, the darts manufacturer revealed Bates’ nickname… ‘The Master’. They tweeted: “Loxley Darts are pleased to announce that Owen “The Master” Bates has joined the team. Owen will be plying his trade on the Development Tour as well as being a regular sparring partner for Ryan Searle.”

    Jay Vargo suggested: “Walk on song is I touch myself???” before asking “who nicknamed this kid?”
