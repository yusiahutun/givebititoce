    Musician Tanasha Donna has heaped praise on her son with Diamond Platnumz, Naseeb Junior.

    In a series of videos shared to her Instagram stories on June 10, 2021, the dotting mother lauded her son for being smart.

    In the videos, Tanasha was the only one in the camera’s frame, but Junior was close by, from his voice.

    Mother and son were heard counting together from one to 20, a move that delighted the Na Wewe singer.

    “Good boy, look at you. Counting to 20 now. Yes, hurray! What a smart kid you are,” Tanasha told her son.

    Animosity

    On June 3, Tanasha proved that they are still not seeing eye to eye as she unfollowed Diamond on Instagram through Junior's account.

    Tanasha eliminated all traces of Diamond on Junior's account by deleting all their photos together.

    While she had already unfollowed the Wasafi head honcho via her account, Diamond is still following her.
