    16 Mar 2024, 00:00

    A model was dumped from the Anthony Mundine vs Danny Green fight not because her 'breasts were too big', but because of her 'attitude', a fellow ring girl claims.

    Sev Philippou said she was initially chosen by promoters as one of four girls to hold up cards inside the ring to let the crowd know which round is about to begin.

    But just hours before the controversial fight, the 25-year-old claimed she was told she was no longer needed after going in for a dress fitting, organised by SA Tourism.

    Sev Philippou (pictured) claimed she was dropped as a ring girl at the controversial boxing bout between Anthony Mundine and Danny Green after being told her 'breasts are too big'

    Leah Matuszek (pictured left with the other two ring girls at the fight) says it was Ms Philippou's attitude that got her cut from the bout

    She claimed it was discrimination for her bra size, but Leah Matuszek, one of the three girls employed for the match, said it was just 'sour grapes'.

    'We were told they wanted natural, professional girls with minimal make-up... but she lacked professionalism and experience. It was her attitude and the way she conducted herself throughout the process,' she told the Adelaide Advertiser. 

    The 20-year-old said bra size was 'definitely not' make-or-break for being selected as she too had large breasts but was successful. 

    The 20-year-old said bra size was 'definitely not' make-or-break for being selected as she too had large breasts but was successful

    The model claimed she was cut from the event after being singled out because of her bust size

    'She lacked professionalism and experience. It was her attitude and the way she conducted herself throughout the process,' Ms Matuszek said of Ms Philippou

    'The selection process itself was very professional and it’s disgraceful to suggest otherwise,' she said. 

    Ms Philippou insisted not only that both the fight promoters and model casting agency told her she was cut because her breasts were too big for the dress, but she was also bustier than Ms Matuszek.
