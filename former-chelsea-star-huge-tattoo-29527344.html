    A former Chelsea player has got a huge tattoo on his head that fans think has 'more ink than a squid.'

    Brazilian ace Kenedy joined the Blues in 2015, and scored three goals in 30 appearances before leaving them in 2022 to join La Liga side Real Valladolid last summer, after a spate of loan spells. Chelsea fans will remember the attacker for the goal he scored against Norwich City after 39 seconds in the Premier League, which was the division's fastest strike that term.

    Since moving to Spain, the 27-year-old's managed to star in five games for the club owned by football great Ronaldo, but his progress was hampered by a slew of injuries.

    Kenedy's latest health issue saw him ruled out for eight to 10 weeks after he tore a hamstring muscle. But he returned to training on Wednesday, and he marked his comeback with a tattoo of what appeared to be Jesus's face that covered his head.

    The left winger's new ink left fans stunned and they flocked to social media to give their opinions. One fan exclaimed: "Mamma Mia, more ink than a squid!" Then another added: "This guy can be more tattooed?"

    A third user quipped: "Gorgeous hairstyle! Very fine the hairdresser with the razor hahaha." And a fourth person reckons the tatt will ensure Valladolid beat the drop as they fight for La Liga survival.

    The user said: "The virgin is on our side, we cannot go down.", in what seemed to be a reference to Jesus' mother Mary. Kenedy's latest tattoo is the latest to cover his heavily inked body.

    He seems to be religious judging by the tattoo on his wrist which has the bible scripture of Hebrews 3:15 on it. And he's also got a tatt of Hollywood icon Marilyn Monroe on his leg that he proudly showed off on Instagram.

    But his club aren't phased by his latest artwork and welcomed him back with open arms on Twitter. They wrote: "Kenedy is one step closer to coming back, Today he has completed the first part of the session with the group."
