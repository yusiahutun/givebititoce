    Whether it’s an annual tradition or a first-time viewing, you may be wondering how to watch the Rose Parade live for free to see the best of the best flower-covered floats. Wondering where to watch the Rose Parade live? Keep reading for the best and one of the only ways to stream it at no cost.

    The Rose Parade, also known as the Tournament of Roses Parade or the Tournament of Roses, is an annual parade held mostly along Colorado Boulevard, in Pasadena, California. The parade, which is usually held on New Year’s Day, includes flower-covered floats, marching bands, and equestrian units and was first held on January 1, 1890.

    Since then, it’s been held uninterrupted except during World War II in 1942, 1943, and 1945, and in 2021 due to the COVID-19 pandemic. In 1902, the Rose Bowl college football game was added as a part of the Tournament of Roses event to help fund the cost of staging the parade. The game, which is considered one of the major bowl games in college football, is held in the afternoon on New Year’s Day at the Rose Bowl in Pasadena, California.

    The Rose Parade, by far, is one of the most historic parades in American history. So where can fans stream the Rose Parade at home this New Year? Read on for how to watch the Rose Parade live for free to see all the extravagant, flower-covered floats come down.
