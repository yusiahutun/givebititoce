    She doesn’t usually shy away from the spotlight, but when driving around, Kim Kardashian likes her privacy.

    So much so that the reality star has gone to great lengths to have the windows of her Mercedes G-Wagon tinted.

    But unfortunately this backfired for Kim on Monday when she was pulled over by police in Studio City.

    Telling off: Kim Kardashian was pulled over by police while driving in Studio City on Monday

    The officers reportedly gave the 32-year-old a telling off after they ruled that her vehicle’s windows were too dark, according to TMZ.

    Fortunately, Kim wasn’t given a ticket and was simply let off with a warning.

    The pregnant TV star had been making her way to her family’s home in Calabasas when the incident occurred.
