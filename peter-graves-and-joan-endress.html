    About

    American Actor Peter Graves was born Peter Duesler Aurness on 18th March, 1926 in Minneapolis, Minnesota, USA and passed away on 14th Mar 2010 Pacific Palisades, California, USA aged 83. He is most remembered for his roles as Jim Phelps in the CBS espionage series Mission: Impossible and the revival series of the same name and Captain Clarence Oveur in the 1980 comedy film Airplane! and its 1982 sequel film Airplane II: The Sequel. His zodiac sign is Pisces.
