    Angelica Guadalupe Celaya is a talented Mexican Actress and is popularly known for her role in Mariposa Del Barrio as Jenni River. She is also nominated for Premios Tu Mundo Award in 2013. Recently in 2021 her show Mariposa De Barrio has been ranked under the top 10 shows in Netflix Mexico.

    Early Life

    Angelica was born on 9th July 1982 in Tucson, Arizona, United States of America. The name of her mother is Lupita Celaya while the information about her father has not been shared with the media. She also has a brother whose name is Jesus Celaya. Her parents belonged from Mexico. She attended Pueblo High School and completed her high school studies. For her higher studies, she went to the University of Arizona. Later she left her studies in order to pursue her career in acting. She also worked hard to become a professional actor.

    Angelica Celaya Career

    She began her career in 2003 by working in an American series named Ladron de Corazones where she played the role of Renata. From 2005 to 2009 she worked in many TV series like Los Plateados, Decisions, Marina, Gabriel, Vivir Por ti and Mientras haya vida. In 2010 she worked in a film named Dead West where she played the role of Gloria Valenzuela and Mas Sabe el diablo: El Primer golpe where her role was of Rene Cardona. The same year she was seen in series named Perro Amor as Miranda, Alguien te Mira as Eva Zanetti, and in Edgar Floats as Penny.
