    Whether it is a game, cinematic piece, play or piece of literature, coming up with a name for your villain characters can be a nightmare. It is just not easy being creative with negative things. Giving a sinister last name to your villain character is integral to showing their intent. In most cases, the character's last name acts as a foreshadowing and reveals them to be a major villain. And there is no better way to do this than going for scary last names. It is all you need to give them a label with which readers and watchers will identify. So, what are the best scary monikers to adopt?

    Last names with dark meanings are perfect for your villain character as it distinguishes them from the rest and announces their evil intentions. Therefore, if you struggle to find a proper scary name for your character, you will be glad to know you could adopt many monikers. Just make sure it fits the intent and brings out the sinister nature of your antagonist well.

    • Mortimer (Latin boy name): This means “dead sea”. It comes from the Latin word “mort,” meaning “death.”
    • Griffin/Gryphon (boy): Mythological beast with the body of a lion and the wings and head of an eagle.
