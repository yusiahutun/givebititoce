    Megan Fox has sent hearts racing yet again over on Instagram, as she poses on the beach in a sheer black dress with a huge chest cut out, showing off her toned body in a tiny silver bikini. With two looks in a short space of time, the star can also be seen modeling another sheer dress, and this time she was baring everything as her make-up artist called it her “new fave look.”

    Since taking a break and resetting her Instagram account, Megan Fox has been the gift that keeps on giving. She’s now taking her bikini pics to the shore, as she calls herself: “cliodhna, queen of the banshees.”

    Megan Fox heats up the beach in tiny silver bikini

    Megan treated her 21 million Instagram followers yet again, and this time, she swapped the jungle leaves for the beach. Although, we’re not sure anyone was paying much attention to the background.

    The Hollywood star posted not one, but six pictures of herself posing in a sheer cut-out dress, as she showed off her curves in a small metallic silver bikini, with the nails to match.

    Fox’s various rib tattoos were also on show, which comes after she covered up her ‘intimate’ tattoo dedicated to her ex.

    The 37-year-old has still kept her striking red locks, which in this shoot, were damp to give her those luscious beach waves.

    Megan’s steamy photoshoot comes after her ‘jungle-inspired’ bikini photoshoot which even had her celebrity partner, MGK drolling in the comments.

    “If this is what a wild animal looks like, I’d let it maul me,” he wrote.

    Fans ask for Megan’s hand in marriage

    Upon seeing the snaps, fans rushed to the comments to compliment the star, some even asking for her hand in marriage.

    “Let’s get married plz,” begged one hopeful fan. We don’t think Meg’s fiance Machine Gun Kelly will be too pleased with that one!

    “You are definitely the most beautiful woman in the world,” penned another.

    Another fan screamed: “MOTHER IS MOTHERING!”

    “This body makes no sense after having kids and above the age of 35. 🥵 I’m jealous,” wrote another fan.

    One simply wrote: “Phenomenal.”

    The actress ‘wows’ fans in mesh dress

    As if Megan Fox wasn’t providing fans with enough content, her makeup artist shared her “new favorite look” as The Jennifer’s Body actress stunned in a sheer orange dress.

    The fit, if anyone was wondering is a Jean Paul Gaultier Striped mesh maxi dress, which costs roughly around $550.

    The actress stunned fans with her perfect makeup, as fans were begging to know the lip color. Her hair was in a neat updo and matched the color of her dress.

    If we had a dollar for every fire emoji in the comments, we’d be rich.
