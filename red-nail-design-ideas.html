    15 Red Nail Art Designs

    1

    Make It Matte

    There's something so alluring about a nail look that doesn't have conventional shine. Choose one finger for some negative space action to add something a little extra.

    Try: Zoya Nail Lacquer in Amal Matte Velvet, $10, zoya.com. SHOP

    2

    Mix And Match

    Red, nude, and lilac make for such a pretty and unexpected color combo. Mix it up further by trying different colors and designs on each finger.

    Try: Lauren B. Beauty in Concrete Jungle, $20, laurenbbeauty.com. SHOP

    3

    Cherry Print

    Upgrade a bare or sheer nail look with the addition of ultra-cutesy cherry nail decals.

    Try: Butter London Patent Shine 10x Nail Lacquer in Her Majesty's Red, $18, dermstore.com. SHOP

    4

    Gold Foil

    The metallic gold foil adds edge to a crimson red manicure.

    Try: Christian Louboutin in Loubi Under Red, $45, sephora.com. SHOP

    5

    Full Bloom

    A flower-inspired design that doesn't look too girly or summery—so it's the perfect transition mani for warmer weather.

    Try: Lauren B. Beauty in Laurel Canyon Lover, $18, laurenbbeauty.com. SHOP

    6

    Bold Tips And Jewels

    If you're looking to up the anté in the nail art game without a ton of effort, just add some jewels.

    Try: OPI Peru Infinite Shine Collection in Como Se Llama, $13, ulta.com. SHOP

    7

    Cardinal & Gold

    We can't get enough of gold foil. If you want something more subtle, apply the metallic shine to one or two nails on each hand.

    Try: CND Shellac UV Color Coat in Wildfire, $16, amazon.com. SHOP

    8

    Negative Space

    The minimalist in us feels seen thanks to the ongoing trend of negative space nails.

    Try: Dior Vernis Gel Shine & Long Wear Nail Lacquer, $28, dior.com. SHOP

    9

    Red Sparkle

    Glitter is an easy way to elevate a plain manicure. Keep it all in the same color family for a dynamic red nail.

    Try: Essie Nail Polish in Roses Are Red, $9, essie.com. SHOP

    10

    Wild Child

    We love a good leopard print. Paint it against a shiny red-orange background to make it really pop.

    Try: CND Shellac Nail Polish in Hollywood, $16, amazon.com. SHOP

    11

    Moon Shine

    The half-moon nail trend will forever be a staple. Dress it up with some gold sparkle to add a modern element.

    Try: JinSOON Nail Lacquer in Pop Orange, $18, nordstrom.com. SHOP

    12

    Paint Splatter

    If you're looking for something a little less polished, take a contrasting color and splatter it all over a red base.

    Try: Essie Nail Polish in Geranium, $9, amazon.com. SHOP

    13

    Geometric Shapes

    This design puts all those years of geometry class to good use.

    Try: Chanel Le Vernis Longwear Nail Colour in Rouge Puissant, $28, nordstrom.com. SHOP

    14

    Throw On Some Flannel

    A plaid nail is our go-to in the fall—but we love it year round as a grunge-inspired red mani option.

    Try: Tom Ford Nail Lacquer in Carnal Red, $37, nordstrom.com. SHOP

    15

    Gradient Nails

    When in doubt, go ombré. Mix red and white together for a striking color combo and add some glitter for a finishing touch.

    Try: NCLA Nail Polish in Rush Hour, $16, thedetoxmarket.com. SHOP
