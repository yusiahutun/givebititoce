    What happened to Putlocker?

    In 2016, the original Putlockers site was blocked because of an order from the High Court in the UK. Motion Picture Association of America reported to the Office of the United States Trade Representative that the site was a piracy threat. From then on, websites that mirrored the original Putlockers site started coming up. On 27th February 2019, a ruling by a Tribunal D'arrondissement of Luxembourg went in favour of the Belgian Entertainment Association resulting in the suspension of their latest site "Putlockers.ch".
