    Kim Kardashian West is famous for many things, one being iconic nude selfies that regularly break the Internet.

    However, according to the KKW Beauty and SKIMS founder herself, the era of Kim K posing naked on Instagram could be coming to an end. In an interview with The Cut, Kardashian West addressed her penchant for posing in her birthday suit and revealed that she's been reconsidering the hobby in recent months.

    "I don't know if it's the fact that my husband has voiced that sometimes too sexy is just overkill and he's not comfortable with that. I listen to him and understand him. Still, at the end of the day, he always gives me the freedom to be and do what I want. But I have kind of had this awakening myself. I realized I could not even scroll through Instagram in front of my kids without full nudity coming up on my feed pretty much all the time. And I definitely contributed to that."

    As well as posing for seriously revealing magazine covers, Kardashian West also realized that her new career as a lawyer might be at odds with her online nudity. "I also did think, like, Okay, I'm here in the White House and then the next day I was posting, like, a crazy bikini selfie. And I was thinking, I hope they don't see this. I have to go back there next week."

    It truly is the end of an era.
