    Make putting pen to paper look incredibly chic this year with our favourite luxury office accessories

      Let's face it, heading to your desk at 9 a.m. with a cup of coffee and getting ready to start another day of work can feel rather monotonous, particularly after the festive holiday shenanigans.

      If a sartorial refresh puts a spring in our step, and flowers immediately add cheerfulness to a room, giving your office an elegant makeover might just help conquer those January office blues.

      There's a plethora of luxury pieces on the market, from reputable stationery brands like Papier to designers like Dior, Celine and Montblanc. Whether you like bold colours, intricate illustrations, personalised stationery or quirky designs, we’ve found the best luxury stationery for the organisational elite.

      So next time you put 'pen to paper, ensure you do so in the chicest way possible

      How we chose the pieces:

      Design: We chose pieces to suit a variety of tastes, so found a range of colours, patterns and aesthetics.

      Price: Luxury products can come with a price tag, but there are many high-quality brands that come at a reasonable price point. So we’ve mixed in some affordable luxury brands with designer gems, depending on budget.

      • Leather Laptop Case

        London Velvet

        The Go-To has the coolest selection of unusual and quirky luxury gifts. This leather laptop case is an elevated basic, crafted from calf leather and padded with a suede lining. Personalise it with your initial to give this piece a touch of cool.

        £325.00 AT THE GO-TO

      • Bespoke Cable Tidy

        Anya Hindmarch

        Is there anything more annoying than wires left loose and getting tangled around your hairbrush? Anya Hindmarch comes to the rescue with this quirky cable tidy that neatly clips onto your bag or keys.

        £140.00 AT ANYA HINDMARCH

      • Large Notebook

        Dior

        For the times when popping out to a meeting and a handbag isn't necessary, use this large Dior bag as your ultra-chic accessory. The Blue Toile de Jouy design is a classic.

        £70.00 AT DIOR

      • Triomphe Bookmark

        Celine

        Quiet Luxury in a bookmark. This brass silver Triomphe piece is truly the perfect addition to your elevated stationery roster.

        £205.00 AT CELINE

      • The Library Spirit Episode London sticky note stationery set

        Montblanc

        Sticky notes just got sophisticated. This London-themed box from Montblanc holds a stack of sticky notes in hues of green and blue. The design is so quirky, no-one would ever know the box is more than a decoration...

        £165.00 AT SELFRIDGES

      • Luxury Letter Rack

        Aspinal of London

        Adding a touch of Aspinal is essential for any sophisticated desk setup. The English heritage label has taken its famous croc handbag pattern and turned it into a vibrant letter rack in a luxurious navy hue.

        £150.00 AT ASPINAL OF LONDON

      • Bespoke 2024 Diary

        London Velvet

        This sophisticated diary is handcrafted in England by classically trained bookbinders. My favourite elements?  the dainty sans serif writing and gold-edged cream paper.

      • Nice and Neat Desk Gift Set

        Papier

        Gift those you love with this cute set from Papier. Complete with a Groove rollerball pen, Rainbow pen clips, Cloud index clips and 9 sheets of planner stickers, they'll have everything they need to stay organised whilst keeping their desk organised.

        £23.80 AT PAPIER

      • Crystalline Nova Rollerball Pen

        Swarovski

        No luxury stationery collection is complete without a dazzling pen. This rose gold Swarovski rollerball is filled with over a thousand clear crystals and is at a seriously good price point in comparison to other high-end ones.

        £60.00 AT SWAROVSKI

      • Desk Mat in Panama

        Smythson

        Did someone say 'quiet luxury'? This sandstone leather desk mat is perfect for giving your study the chicest upgrade. The muted-toned colourway oozes sophistication.

        £595.00 AT SMYTHSON
