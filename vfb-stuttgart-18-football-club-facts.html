    Vfb Stuttgart was founded in 1893

    Let’s start with the beginning. Vfb Stuttgart was established in 1893 and holds the distinction of being one of the oldest football clubs in Germany. From its humble beginnings, the club has grown to become a symbol of football excellence.

    The club has won the Bundesliga title 5 times

    Vfb Stuttgart has stamped its authority on the German football scene, clinching the Bundesliga title on 5 occasions. The club achieved this feat in the years 1950, 1952, 1984, 1992, and 2007, solidifying its place amongst the elites of German football.
