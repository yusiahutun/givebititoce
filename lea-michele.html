    Early Life of Lea Michele

    Lea Michele was born Lea Michele Sarfati on August 29, 1986, in The Bronx, New York of the United States of America. Her age is 36 and her horoscope is Virgo. Lea loves to celebrate her birthday every August 29 with her friends and family. Her parents are Edith Sarfati (father) and Marc Sarfati (mother). Her mother was a retired nurse and her father was a real estate agent.

    Though Lea was born in the Bronx she spent her childhood in the suburbs of New Jersey. Her parental grandparents are Albert Sarfati and Celia/Sylvia Veiss. Michele holds American nationality and belongs to a mixed (Italian and Sephard) ethnic background. She is Catholic.
