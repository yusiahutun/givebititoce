    Meet Alessandra Ambrosios 15-year-old fashionista daughter, Anja who stuns in twinning Gucci

    Alessandras ex-husband and Anjas father is an American entrepreneur who earned his wealth in the clothing business. Once a low-key businessman, his profile rose significantly during his 13-year relationship with the supermodel.

    04 Jun 2024, 00:00
    Born to Alessandra Ambrosio and entrepreneur Jamie Mazur, the teenager – who only celebrated her 15th birthday last August – has already amassed 108,000 Instagram followers at the time of writing.

    Alessandra’s ex-husband and Anja’s father is an American entrepreneur who earned his wealth in the clothing business. Once a low-key businessman, his profile rose significantly during his 13-year relationship with the supermodel.

    Anja’s budding modelling career

    With her growing style and confidence, Anja appears to be following in her mother’s footsteps. Back in 2019, Alessandra revealed to People magazine that 11-year-old Anja had a keen interest in fashion.

    However, in a recent interview with US media, Alessandra shared that Anja is currently focused on high school and not actively pursuing modelling. Nevertheless, she expressed her support for her daughter’s choices, whatever they may be.

    Alessandra and Anja’s shared love of fashion

    The striking resemblance between Anja and her mother is evident in their shared love of fashion. They often pose together, showcasing coordinated looks that run the gamut from red carpet glamour to casual streetwear. Here are just a few of our favourite looks ...

    Seeing red

    For Christmas, the duo donned matching red bodycon dresses, elevating festive fashion to new heights.

    Champagne beauties

    Ringing in the New Year with flair, the mother-daughter duo looked stunning in champagne-coloured mini dresses: Alessandra in a sparkly, long-sleeved fringe dress, and Anja in a strapless, ruched dress.

    Summer dresses at sunset

    Anja’s 15th birthday was marked with a photo shoot in breezy summer dresses, set against a beautiful sunset.

    Fringe fabulous

    In another special moment, the mother-daughter pair showcased their individual flair in matching fringed dresses, with Alessandra opting for elegant high heels and Anja rocking Converse trainers.

    In a 2022 interview with Harper’s Bazaar, Alessandra emphasised the importance of her children finding their own style, saying, “I want them to feel happy and comfortable in their own skin, so I would gently guide them if I felt they were trying to be someone they’re not.”

    Gucci fam

    The Ambrosio family’s love of fashion was on full display at a São Paulo Gucci party. Alessandra turned heads in a dazzling white gown with a deep neckline, while Anja stylishly paired a bustier top with pink satin pyjamas.
