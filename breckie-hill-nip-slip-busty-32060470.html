    Breckie Hill – the foe of LSU college star Olivia Dunne – suffered an embarrassing wardrobe malfunction during a live stream.

    The 20-year-old social media personality has become a popular figure on TikTok over the last couple of years thanks to her viral dancing and lip-sync videos. It has seen her audience to over 3.2million followers and 124.5m likes.

    Some of Breckie’s videos have seen her aim jabs at millionaire Louisiana State University athlete Olivia Dunne, who is the face of college athletics in the US, starring for the LSU Tigers gymnastics team. While it’s unknown when the two first came to blows, their dislike for each other is obvious.
