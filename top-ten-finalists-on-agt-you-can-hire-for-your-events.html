    Top Ten Finalists On AGT You Can Hire For Your Events

    You only need to watch one episode of America’s Got Talent to realize that the show features some of the most talented performers from all over the country.

    Comedians, magicians, singers, mentalists, bands, jugglers, impressionists, vocal acts, acrobats, you name it – AGT has got it all.

    Our purpose is to provide the best entertainment possible, which is why today we will talk about ten finalists on AGT which you can hire for your events.

    Let’s discover them one by one.

  • Joe Castillo

    • Joe Castillo is a sand animation artist. He developed this new method of telling stories by using sand art, a light table, music and, of course, his hands.

    He creates different scenes which are displayed on a screen which the audience can see at all times. Joe’s gift is to take any message, including for corporate events, and make it resonate with the audience through the art he creates.

  • Recycled Percussion

    • Recycled Percussion is a percussion group act, and since 1995, they have been unstoppable. Their live performance is something you have never seen before.

    The band which is formed by three drummers and one DJ keeps the audience on their feet with their signature “trash” kits, including plastic buckets, huge ladders, mortar casings, large 50-gallon containers and amazing beatbox.

  • Evie Clair

    • Evie Clair is an incredibly talented singer who is only 14 years old. She dedicated her performance on America’s Got Talent to her father who battled with colon cancer and sadly passed away.

    She not only can perform covers of favorite songs such as Thinking Out Loud by Ed Sheeran, but she also released two original pop songs before she turned 12.

  • Preacher Lawson

    • Preacher Lawson is one of the first finalists on AGT from this list who is a comedian. Thanks to his goofiness and high-energy comedy, he brings to life charismatic and animated characters.

    Preacher’s energy and clean humor can keep the audience laughing on their toes throughout his entire performance.

  • Drew Lynch

    • Drew Lynch suffered a softball injury which left him with a severe stutter, but this did not stop him from becoming a performer on stage. Even though he wanted to be an actor, after the accident, he turned to stand-up comedy.

    Now, he is a successful comedian who managed to win the hearts of the AGT jury and the audience in the tenth season.

  • Sara Carson

    • Sara Carson is one of the top trick dog instructors in the world, and over the years she has earned several agility titles. She became well-known from appearing on The Late Show with David Letterman.

    Hero, a Border Collie, is the name of the dog Sara performs with. When Hero was only four months old, he became the youngest champion trick dog in the world. Now, Hero knows hundreds of tricks which will most likely blow your mind.

  • Passing Zone

    • Passing Zone is a comedy juggler duo act formed by Jon Wee and Owen Morse. Not only do they have amazing juggling skills, but they also deliver quality entertainment and an unforgettable experience.

    Their shows consist of roaring chainsaws, cheering attendees, flaming torches and knives flying through the air.


  • Derek Hughes

    • Derek Hughes is a one of a kind theatrical magician and comedian. Even though he performed quite a lot on television, his primary love is represented by live performances.

    Derek is a true master of conjuring and thanks to his sense of humor and mind-blowing tricks; he quickly became one of the most sought-after entertainers in the country.

  • Alicia Michilli

    • Alicia Michilli is an R&B/Soul singer. Her idols are Etta James, Aretha Franklin, Ella Fitzgerald, and Billie Holiday, and you can spot the influences in her music.

    Thanks to her appearance on AGT, she gained much exposure and even managed to perform with her hero, Keb’Mo, an American blues musician and four-time Grammy award winner.

  • AcroArmy

    • AcroArmy is an acrobatic dance troupe which has reached the highest levels of achievement in acrobatic gymnastics.

    Every one of the 17 members of the group has been selected to represent the US on the National Team and have earned many national and international titles.

    Now, they are an elite performance team which will blow your mind.

    Wow Your Guests by Booking Finalists on AGT

    We made sure to feature in this article finalists on AGT which have various backgrounds. That way, you can find an entertainer who can suit the type of event you are organizing and the atmosphere you want to create.
