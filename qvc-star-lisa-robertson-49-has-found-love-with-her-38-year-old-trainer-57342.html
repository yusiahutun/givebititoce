    Just more than four months after Lisa Robertson announced she was leaving QVC, the star has stepped out with a new boyfriend!

    Lisa, 49, has reportedly been dating her 38-year-old trainer, Eric McGee, for several months but kept their relationship a secret until she quit her 20-year hosting gig on the home shopping network in December 2014.

    Lisa with Eric in April 2015.

    Eric, a divorced father-of-two, has allegedly already met the TV personality’s family and friends. A recent influx of posts made by Lisa about her new love on her social media accounts have additionally led many of her fans to speculate the happy couple may marry soon.

    On Valentine’s Day, she posted a cute photo of her being carried on Eric’s back on her Facebook page with the caption, “Love is someone who will carry you when the stones hurt your feet.”

    Lisa with Eric in February 2015.

    After wrapping the taping of her final appearance on QVC, Lisa similarly took to social media to thank her loyal viewers for all their years of support.

    “I am not sure you realize what a difference you make. We have shared a lot you and I, the good and the bad,” she wrote. “You have become a part of my every day. I know you listen to me, but I want you to know that I listen to you, too. You have been a bigger help, a greater source of comfort and a more welcome diversion when I have needed it, than anything else I can think of.”
