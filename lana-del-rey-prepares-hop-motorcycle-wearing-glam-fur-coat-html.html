    Though her fashion style is often a mixture of high-end and hipster pieces, Lana Del Rey hardly looks like a biker girl.

    So the Video Games singer found herself dressed somewhat unusually as she got ready to hop onto a friend's motorcycle last night in Los Angeles.

    The 25-year-old chanteuse may have been the most fashionable girl to ever climb on a road hog, given that she was wearing a glamorous fur coat.

    Biker chic: Lana Del Rey prepared to hop on a friend's motorcycle last night after attending an event in Los Angeles

    The rest of her outfit was one of her typically quirky combinations.

    She paired an elegant, yet busy, button-down shirt with a thick gold necklace.

    Her wet look jeans fit tightly in all the right places, yet she eschewed sexy heels for her favourite Converse trainers.

    Furry fashion: Lana Del Rey attended a private event in Los Angeles last night

    Though her face was pared-down and virtually make-up free, she had on extra-long fake nails.

    The singer - whose real name is Lizzy Grant - looked a little nervous as she covered up her lustrous red mane with a helmet.

    She prepared to hop on the bike with Justin Murdock, heir to the Dole fortune.

    The two had arrived in a car to attend the UCLA Institute of the Environment and Sustainability Presents An Evening of Environmental Excellence at a private home in LA.

    What a stunner! The 25-year-old singer has a contradictory - yet appealing - fashion style

    Her boyfriend, Kassidy band member Barrie James O'Neil, was nowhere to be seen.

    In other news, the singer recently taped a performance on American Idol.

    And by all accounts, it's meant to be quite spectacular, as she's accompanied by a 16-piece orchestra and piano.

    The performance will air on the hit Fox show March 22.
