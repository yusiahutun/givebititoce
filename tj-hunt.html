    Biography Timeline

    TJ Hunt is a famous American YouTuber and automotive enthusiast known for his passion for cars and content creation. With a substantial following on social media, Hunt has become a prominent figure in the automotive world. Here is a detailed biography timeline of TJ Hunt.
    • 1994: TJ Hunt was born on December 28th in Montreal, Canada.
    • 2011: He moved to the United States and settled in California.
    • 2014: Hunt started his YouTube channel, initially creating videos about his life and interests.
    • 2015: He gained popularity for his automotive content, showcasing his car builds and modifications.
    • 2016: TJ Hunt launched his aftermarket parts company, Hunt&Co, specialising in automotive accessories and apparel.
    • 2017: He partnered with fellow YouTuber Adam LZ to create a car build series called “Drift My Dad’s Car.”
    • 2018: Hunt collaborated with Hoonigan, an automotive lifestyle brand, on a limited-edition apparel collection.
    • 2019: He began hosting a podcast called “Huntquarters” with his girlfriend, Daniella.
    • 2020: TJ Hunt sold Hunt&Co to focus on content creation, which has grown to over 1.8 million subscribers on YouTube.
    • 2021: He launched a new brand, Alpha Supply Co., which offers automotive-inspired clothing and accessories.
    TJ Hunt’s dedication to the automotive industry and passion for creating content have earned him a massive following on social media. He has expanded his brand through Hunt&Co and Alpha Supply Co. and continues to produce popular videos on his YouTube channel. With a solid fan base and a growing brand, Hunt has established himself as a leading figure in the automotive world.
