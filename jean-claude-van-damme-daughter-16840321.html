    Our Instagram Babe of the Week title goes to Bianca Van Damme.

    The only daughter of film icon Jean-Claude Van Damme, 57, is taking the internet by storm, one sizzling snap at a time.

    By no means an easy task, the stunning brunette has racked up over 86.2k followers on the photo-sharing site.

    So just what do we know about the Californian girl?

    While the starlet's birth is Bianca Van Varenberg, she uses her dad's famous moniker for her Instagram page.

    However, when it comes to her professional work, the starlet now uses the surname "Bree".

    Far from trying to leave her famous dad's shadow, Bianca has followed in his footsteps.

    The 27-year-old actress made her acting debut in 2008 blockbuster The Shepherd, alongside JCVD himself.

    Proving themselves to be a good team, the duo also joined forces for 2011 film The Assassin.

    In Bianca's Instagram bio, she introduces herself with: "I kick and punch s***."

    Instagram

    Jean-Claude is famous for his action hero status, using his prowess in marital arts to dominate the screen.

    Bianca has continued her dad's legacy, becoming a martial arts expert in her own right.

    The starlet undoubtedly comes from an athletic background on both sides, as her mum Gladys Portugues is a former body builder and fitness coach.

    Seemingly aware of her bombshell status, Bianca is known to send fans into a frenzy as she flaunts her toned physique in scantily-clad attires.

    Instagram

    In one sizzling photoshoot, the US beauty poured her assets into a high-cut swimsuit, which clung to her body like a second skin.

    Revealing the pictures had been inspired by her mum, she captioned the shoot with the hashtags "Fitness model", "80s", "Gladys Portugues" and "mini-me".

    Responding to the update, one admirer quipped: "From girl next door to beach bomb," alongside several heart-eyed emojis.

    In agreement, one viewer wrote: "My god, you're hot!"

    For another daring moment, Bianca posed topless on the beach, wearing nothing but a pair of tiny bikini bottoms.

    With her abs of steel taking pride of place, the buxom babe left fans hot under the collar.

    "A wonderful and enchanting beauty," an awestruck follower marvelled.

    Although Bianca isn't known for chasing the spotlight as public events, she was pictured at the Paris premiere of JCVD's new Amazon TV show Jean-Claude Van Johnson.

    Daily Star Online was also in attendance at the star-studded event, as JC unveiled his latest on-screen persona.
