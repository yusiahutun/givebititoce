    Nadine Velazquez is an American actress and model who has become popular through her numerous works, including her appearances in major productions such as Major Crimes and My Name Is Earl. Here you can find out everything about her.

    Nadine Velazquez Biography

    She was born on November 20, 1978, in Chicago, Illinois, as Nadine Velazquez and is from Puerto Rico. Little is known about her parents, her family, and her upbringing.

    She seems to have always had a passion for acting and modeling, as she is said to have tried it herself at the age of 13 when she went to a talent agency but didn’t make it because she wasn’t supported by her parents at the time.

    For her training, the multi-talented actress attended Notre Dame High School for girls, and from there she went to Columbia College Chicago, where she graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Marketing.

    Before she had her breakthrough as an actress, the gorgeous Latina started out as a model. She got her first job as a professional actress in 2003 when she got a role in The Bold and the Beautiful, which was followed by the Biker Boyz. In 2004, she starred in the film Blast and in several television shows; The Last Ride and Entourage.

Nadine Velazquez’s most recent works are Ride Along 2, The Bounce Back, and Z Nation, all produced in 2016, and Six, A World Away, Sharon 1.2.3. and Our Bnb Life, produced in 2017.

During the Alliance of Women Film Journalists awards ceremony, she received the award for the most significant age difference between the leading man and the love interest.

    During the Miss Universe 2008 and 2009 elections, Velazquez played an active role by participating as judge and host, respectively. She was also involved in a number of commercials in various media.
