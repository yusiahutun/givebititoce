    1. How many calories are in a serving of Toblerone?

    A single serving of Toblerone, which is approximately 35 grams, contains around 180 calories.

    2. Is Toblerone gluten-free?

    No, Toblerone contains gluten as it includes ingredients such as wheat flour and barley malt extract. It is not suitable for individuals with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease.

    3. What is the fat content in Toblerone?

    A serving of Toblerone contains approximately 10 grams of fat, including saturated fats. It’s important to consume Toblerone in moderation due to its high fat content.

    4. Does Toblerone contain nuts?

    Yes, Toblerone may contain traces of nuts, such as almonds, due to the manufacturing process. It is not recommended for individuals with nut allergies.

    Yes, Toblerone is suitable for vegetarians as it does not contain any ingredients derived from animals.

    6. Is Toblerone a good source of antioxidants?

    Toblerone contains cocoa, which is known for its antioxidant properties. However, it is important to note that the amount of cocoa in Toblerone may vary depending on the specific variant.

    A serving of Toblerone contains approximately 16 grams of sugar. This is a significant amount, so it should be enjoyed in moderation as part of a balanced diet.

    No, most Toblerone variants are not suitable for vegans as they contain milk and may also contain honey. However, Toblerone does offer a vegan-friendly variant that is made with dark chocolate and no animal products.

    While Toblerone can be stored in the refrigerator, it is recommended to keep it in a cool, dry place to maintain its texture and prevent moisture from affecting its quality.
