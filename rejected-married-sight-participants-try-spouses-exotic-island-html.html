    The 16 unlucky in love singles who are appearing on the Married at First Sight spin-off series Married at First Sight: Honeymoon Island are a mix of fan favorites, unmatched candidates from previous seasons, and new faces.

    While Married at First Sight participants say 'I do' as strangers, the cast of the new Lifetime reality series have until the end of their stay to decide whether they want to get married or leave the island as single as they were when they arrived.

    Going for it: Sixteen unlucky in love singles are searching for spouses on Married at First Sight: Honeymoon Island, a spin-off of the Lifetime reality series Married at First Sight 

    Although the concept of the spin-off series is entirely new, many of the men and women who are seeking spouses on the island have already auditioned for Married at First Sight.

    While they were initially willing to marry perfect strangers, the show's experts either failed to find them matches or they ended up getting cold feet and backing out of the process.   

    Fans of the franchise will also be quick to recognize Isabella McKenzie and Shannon Raddler. The two tried to win the hearts of David Norton and Vanessa Nelson on Married at First Sight: Second Chances.  

    There are also plenty of new faces on Honeymoon Island, but they all share the common belief that the show is their second chance at finding love.

    First up: Travis Dalton is a 30-year-old from Bluffton, South Carolina

    Not deterred: The aerospace engineer watched his sister Tara get her heartbroken by David Norton on Married at First Sight: Second Chances

    On the hunt: Travis said he is looking for a 'great love' to share his life with 

    Travis Dalton 

    The 30-year-old from Bluffton, South Carolina, watched his sister Tara get her heartbroken by David Norton on Married at First Sight: Second Chances, but that didn't stop him from signing up for Honeymoon Island.

    The aerospace engineer said watching his sister find love on the show made him realize that you just never know where you will find the one. 

    Like most of the show's participants, he is looking for a 'great love' to share his life with, and he is hopeful that he will meet her on the island.

    All smiles: Isabella McKenzie is a 25-year-old hostess from Atlanta, Georgia

    Fan favorite: She made it to the top two while vying for David Norton's heart on Married at First Sight: Second Chances

    Optimistic: Isabella sees Honeymoon Island as another opportunity for her to find lasting love

    Isabella McKenzie

    The 25-year-old from Atlanta, Georgia, made it to the top two while vying for David Norton's heart on Married at First Sight: Second Chances, but in the end, they weren't the right match.  

    Isabella admits she has high standards, but she believes she is still single because she hasn't met anyone interesting enough to commit to.

    The hostess is tired of being alone, and she sees Honeymoon Island as another opportunity for her to find lasting love. 

    Seasoned bachelor: Bryan McKay is a 40-year-old estimator from Miami, Florida

    Holding out: Bryan believes he is still single because he yet to meet a woman who is the complete package for him

    Bryan McKay 

    The 40-year-old from Miami, Florida, was close to saying 'I do' to a stranger on season four of Married at First Sight, but his match dropped out of the show at the last minutes because her family disapproved. 

    Bryan admittedly prefers to meet women in person because he doesn't think his personality and sense of humor translate as well online or on dating apps.

    The estimator believes he is still single because he yet to meet a woman who is the complete package for him. He hopes Ms. Right will be waiting for him on the island.

    New Englander: Lizzie Muse is a 30-year-old Kundalini yoga teacher from Boston, Massachusetts

    Hard times: The team of experts on Married at First Sight struggled to find her a match when she auditioned for the sixth season of the show

    Lizzie Muse 

    The 30-year-old from Boston, Massachusetts, has gone great lengths to find love to no avail. After failing to find the right match on dating apps and sites, Lizzie hired a dating coach, but the person was unable to find her the right partner. 

    Even the team of experts on Married at First Sight struggled to find her a match when she auditioned for the sixth season of the show. 

    The Kundalini yoga believes Honeymoon Island is the universe's way of giving her a second chance at finding the love of her life.

    Strike a pose: Shannon Raddler is a 33-year-old pharmaceutical sales rep from Atlanta, Georgia

    Not meant to be: He appeared on Married at First Sight: Second Chances, but he wasn't ready to pop the question to Vanessa Nelson at the end of filming

    Second chance? Shannon hopes he will find the love of his life on the island 

    Shannon Raddler  

    The 33-year-old from Atlanta, Georgia, fell for Vanessa Nelson while watching season three of Married at First Sight, but it wasn't meant to be.

    After he auditioned for Married at First Sight: Second Chances, he ended up coming in the top two, but he ultimately wasn't ready to pop the question at the end of filming. 

    The pharmaceutical sales rep is hopeful that the person he is meant to share his life with turns out to be one of his new castmates.

    Sunshine state: Mekalya Silvera is a 32-year-old hair stylist from Miami, Florida

    Missing soulmate? She was close to marrying a stranger on season four of Married at First Sight, but her groom got cold feet and dropped out of the process

    All you need is love: Mekalya believes she is destined to meet the man of her dreams on Honeymoon Island 

    Mekalya Silvera 

    The 32-year-old from Miami, Florida, was close to marrying a stranger on season four of Married at First Sight, but her groom got cold feet and dropped out of the process at the last minute.   

    Mekalya initially feared she missed out on marrying her soulmate, but she now believes the real reason she never ended up meeting her expert chosen groom was that she is destined to meet the man of her dreams on Honeymoon Island.

    When it comes to finding love on reality television, the hair stylist thinks the second time will be the charm for her.

    Success: Corey Saunders is the 31-year-old founder and CEO of BEFIT

    Ladies man: He was named Boston's Most Eligible Bachelor in 2017

    Cold feet: Corey auditioned for the sixth season of Married at First Sight, but he backed out during the casting process

    Corey Saunders

    The 31-year-old founder and CEO of BEFIT was named Boston's Most Eligible Bachelor in 2017, but that hasn't made finding love any easier for him. 

    Corey's business and career have been his main focus in the past, but now he feels he is ready to find love once and for all.  

    He auditioned for the sixth season of Married at First Sight, but he backed out during the casting process because he was uncomfortable with having little control over the matchmaking process. 

    The businessman hopes his appearance on Honeymoon Island will lead him to the woman of his dreams.

    Southern lady: Brandin Brosh is a 28-year-old boutique owner from Biloxi, Mississippi

    Heartbreak: She and her boyfriend of three and a half years split up after she caught him cheating on her 

    No Southern charm: Brandin insists the good guys in Mississippi either end up moving away after college or getting married young

    Brandin Brosh

    The 28-year-old from Biloxi, Mississippi, has had trouble finding love after she broke up with her unfaithful college boyfriend. 

    Brandin considered her former beau of three and a half years her best friend and believed they would eventually get married. However, she swiftly changed the locks on her home and broke up with him via text after she caught him cheating on her.

    The clothing boutique owner partially blames her home state for her relationship status, explaining the good guys in Mississippi either end up moving away after college or getting married young.  

    Like many of the other participants, she believes Mr. Right will be waiting for her on Honeymoon Island. 

    Working man: Jona Bienko is a 35-year-old auto broker from Como, Colorado

    Disappointment: Jona applied for season four of Married at First Sight, but he never made it on to the series

    Focused: Getting married and starting a family is a top priority for Jona 

    Jona Bienko 

    The 35-year-old from Como, Colorado, applied for season four of Married at First Sight, but he never made it on to the series.  

    Within minutes of finding out that the experts had found him a match, he had learned that the woman had already withdrawn from the process.   

    Despite the disappointment, Jona was impressed by how much attention the experts paid to find him the perfect spouse. 

    Getting married and starting a family is a top priority for the auto broker, who hopes his dream girl will be waiting for him on Honeymoon Island. 

    Texan: Jada Rashawn is a 27-year-old nanny placing specialist from San Antonio, Texas

    Good idea: She recently took three years off from dating after a breakup to focus on herself

    Confidence: Jada knows what she has to offer in a relationship, and she is hoping to find Mr. Right on the island

    Jada Rashawn

    The 27-year-old from San Antonio, Texas, is looking for the man of her dreams and unwilling to settle for the wrong guy.

    Jada took three years off from dating after a breakup to focus on herself, but now she is ready to try and find love.

    The professional nanny placement specialist and brand spokesperson is confident that she knows what she has to offer in a relationship when she meets Mr. Right â€” she just has to find him.

    Fit: Chris Perry is a 29-year-old personal trainer from Binghamton, New York

    Not a fan: The musician has trouble on dating apps because women think he is catfishing them

    Awww: He wants to meet a woman that he can bring home to meet his mother

    Chris Perry 

    The 29-year-old personal trainer from Binghamton, New York, typically meets girls at the gym, but he admits that has trouble on dating apps because women tend to think he is catfishing them. 

    Chris, who claims to be a romantic at heart, believes his longest relationship of two years to be a trial run for marriage. He wants to meet a woman that he can spoil and bring home to meet his mother.

    The musician and youth counselor hopes his appearance on Honeymoon Island will ensure that he will never have to use a dating app again.

    Independent woman: Jocelyn Thompson is a 30-year-old business owner from Los Angeles, California

    No go: The entrepreneur has struggled to find guys on dating apps and sites 

    Match made in heaven: Jocelyn is admittedly a huge fan of Married at First Sight

    Jocelyn Thompson 

    The 30-year-old business owner from Los Angeles, California, feels the only thing missing from her life is a partner with whom she can share her successes. 

    Now that she is in her 30s, Jocelyn knows she only has so much time left if she wants to have children. However, she has been unable to find the right guy using dating apps.

    She is admittedly a huge fan of Married at First Sight, particularly the relationship that formed between season one stars Cortney Hendrix and Jason Carrion, and she is excited about the possibility of finding love on a similar show.

    Out west: Eric Acosta is a 31-year-old entrepreneur from Denver, Colorado

    Trouble: His last relationship ended after a year and a half, partially because of the amount of traveling he has to do for work

    He believes in love: Eric believes he is a hopeless romantic and a dreamer

    Eric Acosta

    The 31-year-old entrepreneur from Denver, Colorado, considers himself a hopeless romantic and dreamer, so he was naturally intrigued by the Married at First Sight process.

    Eric's last relationship ended after a year and a half, partially because of the amount of traveling he has to do for work. 

    As someone who believes love can find you anywhere, at any time, he is ready to let his heart lead the way on Honeymoon Island.

    She's a success: Katie Muller is a 28-year-old from Denver, Colorado, who has career in digital advertising

    Strong enough to be her man? As an adventure seeker, she is looking for a guy who can keep up with her 

    Missing out: Katie typically meets men through friends and dating apps, but none of them have been the one

    Katie Muller

    The 28-year-old from Denver, Colorado, has her own place and a successful career in digital advertising, but she still hasn't met Mr. Right. 

    Katie is an adventure seeker who is looking for a guy who is as dynamic as she is, and while she typically meets men through friends and dating apps, none of them have been the one. 

    For her, love is all-consuming, and she is hoping to find the man of her dreams on Honeymoon Island.

    Career: Tyler Moody is a 26-year-old construction company owner from Greeley, Colorado

    Hard to find: He has established himself in his career, but he has yet to meet a woman that he desires

    Going for it: Tyler believes the chance of finding love on Honeymoon Island is worth the risk

    Tyler Moody

    The 26-year-old from Greeley, Colorado, is a risk taker who believes the timing of Honeymoon Island couldn't be any more perfect.

    As a construction company owner, Tyler has established himself in his career, but he has yet to meet a woman that he desires and wants to share his life with.

    He believes the chance of finding love on Honeymoon Island is worth the risk, and he is looking forward to meeting the partner of his dreams.

    Last but not least: Kimber Gardner is a 28-year-old from Las Vegas, Nevada

    Busy woman: The medical sales rep is looking or a man who is as career-focused and motivated as she is

    Worth a shot: Kimber believes she will be able to find true love on Honeymoon Island 

    Kimber Gardner

    The 28-year-old from Las Vegas, Nevada, is looking to find a man who is just as career-focused and motivated as she is. 

    Kimber believes that if couples are able to meet their perfect spouses on Married at First Sight, she will be able to find true love on Honeymoon Island.

    The medical sales rep hopes her competitive nature will translate to life on the island and help her bag the man of her dreams.
