    A woman shared her latest Amazon finds and upgraded her car using two gadgets.

    Katie, from the US, posted on TikTok and said how she saved time fiddling with car setups when using satellite navigation or trying to play music from her phone.

    The two tech pieces are great to use on older car models that don't come with an interactive screen, it's the UWECAN Wireless Adapter and the Hikity Front and Back Wireless Car Camera.

    She bought the adapter in white and explained: "This gadget allows you to use Apple CarPlay wirelessly. Instead of having to connect your phone to a USB every time you get in the car, you can use this adapter and pair it with your car and your cellphone.

    "You can wirelessly access all the CarPlay features, plus the passengers can connect to it and share music and directions.

    "It's compact so you can travel with it or store it when not in use."

    The £49.99 adapter also works on Android phones as the listing gives you options for either Android phones or iPhones.

    Then she clipped the wireless car camera onto her rear view mirror and said it has more functions than just a camera.

    "This rear view mirror has two cameras, Apple CarPlay, Android Auto and more. It installs right over an existing mirror for a universal fit and has an adjustable front dash cam, plus a rear view camera," she continued.

    "You can install separately on the back of your car. Since it pairs with the Bluetooth on your phone, you can access Apple CarPlay and Android Auto wirelessly, even if your car doesn't have those features.

    "If also has a split display so you can use GPS navigation and music at once and you can easily switch back to a regular mirror at the push of a button.",

    There is a slot on the side where she put a memory card to store all camera footage.

    She scrolled the smart screen at the touch of a finger and opened Google Maps, Spotify at the same time.

    The 11.26 inch smart rear view mirror is now on 30% off and shoppers can buy it for £69.99 (normally costs £99.99).

    Alternatively, shoppers can go for this Mirror Dual Dash Cam, £18.99, that comes with a pop-up screen to capture everything outside the car while intergrating with the existing rear view mirror.

    Halfords also sells a dashcam that works on a rearview mirror for £199.99.
