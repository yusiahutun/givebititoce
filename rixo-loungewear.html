    Rixo loungewear is here to spruce up your at-home wardrobe

    27 Mar 2024, 00:00

    With another national lockdown well underway and Christmas lounging on the horizon, there could be no better time to treat yourself to a new pair of pyjamas. So, if you are in the market for elevating your at-home wardrobe, then let us direct you to an exciting new launch, Rixo's first loungewear line.

    The British brand, which celebrated its five-year anniversary last month, has today announced the release of its first collection of loungewear and pyjamas, which embodies the same vintage spirit that the label has become so well known for.

    Courtesy of Rixo

    Related Story

    The collection features button-up shirt-and-trouser sets, nightshirts and dresses, T-shirts, a robe, a beautiful embroidered shirt and a number of eye masks. Many of the pieces are covered in some of Rixo's most well-known prints, from stars to ditsy florals, while the T-shirt is emblazoned with the positive slogan: 'Eat, Sleep, Be Happy, Repeat'.

    Designed to help "have you wake up happy and wear all day too", the collection provides the perfect pick-me-up to your pyjama collection, whether you are wearing them to sleep in, popping them on to lounge around in during the Christmas break or even making them part of your new working-from-home uniform.

    Rixo's first loungewear collection is available to shop now, with prices ranging from £55 to £195.
