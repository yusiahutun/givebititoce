    1. Wizkid - $20 million

    Ayodeji Ibrahim Balogun, also referred to as Wizkid, is the richest artist in Nigeria with a net worth of $20 million. The musician is renowned globally and is likely the most successful, decorated, and revolutionary Afro-pop artist in the modern era.

    Wizkid's first release, Holla at Your Boy, launched in 2010, catapulted him to the limelight. The song became part of his first studio album Superstar that was released in 2011. The artist started his record label Starboy. He is the first African artist to enter the Guinness Book of World Records.
